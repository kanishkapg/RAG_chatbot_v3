CEO’s Circular No: 14/2023

CEO’s Office, Sri Lanka Telecom PLC, Colombo 01.

22nd February 2023

To: All SLT Employees,

SALARY INCREASE FOR THE YEAR 2023

SLT Management is pleased to inform you that the Board of Directors has approved the salary increase up to a maximum of 8% for the year 2023 in following manner to all SLT permanent staff (executive & non-executive staff) and to the employees who were recruited on contract basis from HCS to SLT (under the intake of June 2020), with effect from 01st January 2023 subject to the other conditions stipulated in the “Annual Salary Revision, Bonus & Leave Encashment Procedure” of SLT. However, employees who have taken unauthorized no pay leave during the year 2022 will not be entitled for the Annual Salary increase.

a. 6.5% Salary Increase (Fixed portion).

b. Up to 1.5% Salary Increase based on the individual performances in line with the criteria detailed below.

| Performance Rating | Percentage of Salary Increase (Individual Performance Based Increase) |
|---|---|
| 5    | 1.5 %    |
| 4    | 1.2 %    |
| 3    | 0.9 %    |
| 2    | 0.6 %    |
| 1    | 0 %    |

The management of SLT is grateful on the initiatives taken by all members of the staff in improving productivity during the last year. We look forward to your fullest support in the future as well.

Janaka R. Abeysinghe
Chief Executive Officer
