CEO’s Circular No: 63/2023

CEO’s Office, Sri Lanka Telecom PLC, Colombo 01.
4th September 2023

To All SLT Staff,

Internal Recruitment of Marketing Officer-A9

Applications are invited from permanent SLT employees who possess following qualifications and fulfill other eligibility criteria as at 31st August 2023 for the post stated.

1. Qualifications & Eligibility Criteria for the post of Marketing Officer-A9;  
   a) G.C.E. (O/L) Examination with 06 passes at one sitting with four credit passes including Sinhala / Tamil, English Language and Mathematics.  
    AND  
   b) A Diploma in Marketing from SLIM (Graduate Diploma Level)/CIM Part II or Advanced Diploma in Marketing from a University/Institute recognized by UGC-Sri Lanka or any other government authorities, equivalent to NVQ-6 or SLQF 4 or above.  
    AND  
   c) Three years of permanent, confirmed and satisfactory service in SLT as at the date specified above.

2. Method of Selection:  
   Selection will be made through a written examination (English Medium). Only those who achieve required competency at the examination will be called to interview for final selection. Placement will be done on par with the organizational operational exigency.

3. Method of Applying:  
   Application should be submitted through ERP System to reach HR Strategies & Policy Section within 10 days of issuing this circular. Scan copies of relevant certificates should be attached.

ERP System>SLT Employee Self-Service>Other Requests>Internal Recruitments>Click on ‘Create Application’>Select the Post Applied> Attach Relevant Certificates>Add Qualifications>Click on ‘Create’>Click on ‘Application Number’>tick the ‘Declaration’ >Submit.  
(Application will be submitted to the HR after recommendation of the immediate executive supervisor).

Applications sent without supporting documents will not be entertained. Only shortlisted applicants will be contacted by HR.

Janaka R Abeysinghe  
Chief Executive Officer
