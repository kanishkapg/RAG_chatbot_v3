To All Staff,

PROMOTION FROM SENIOR SUPERVISOR (TECHNICAL) – A11 TO TELECOMMUNICATION TECHNICAL OFFICER – A10

Applications are invited from Senior Supervisor (Technical)-A11 Grade employees, who satisfy the under mentioned requirements to be considered for the above promotion.

A. ELIGIBILITY

Candidates should have completed three (03) years' service as Senior Supervisor (Technical)-A11 Grade in SLT as at 30th April 2024

B. SELECTION METHOD

1. Selection will be made through an examination and an interview.
2. The candidates should select their preferred language to sit for the examination (Sinhala/Tamil/English).
3. Interview will be held only for successful candidates at the examination

The subjects specified for the examination are as follows:
i) Mathematics and Analytical skills (1 hour)
ii) Subject Knowledge
- Part I - Multiple Choice Questions (45 minutes)
- Part II - Essay Type Questions (1 ¼ hours)

C. PLACEMENT

Selected candidates will be promoted as TTO A10 and placed in the vacant positions. Minimum of four (04) years should be served at the deployed station to be eligible to request for a Transfer.

D. METHOD OF APPLYING

Eligible candidates should fill in the attached application form and forward to DGM/ HR Operations, 7th Floor, HQ Building, Sri Lanka Telecom PLC, Lotus Road, Colombo 01 through the executive supervising officer (A5 or above).

E. CLOSING DATE

Applications should be sent within seven (07) working days of issuing this circular. Applications sent after the closing date will not be considered.

Janaka R Abeysinghe
Chief Executive Officer
