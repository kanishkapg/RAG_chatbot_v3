GROUP FORENSICS & COMPLIANCE POLICY

The "Group Forensics & Compliance Policy" outlines the procedures and guidelines for conducting forensic investigations and overseeing compliance processes throughout the SLTMobitel group. This policy applies to all employees, contractors, vendors, and third parties associated with the SLTMobitel group, who are involved in activities that may impact the company's compliance, financial integrity, and reputation.

This policy has been approved by the Board Audit Committee of the SLTMobitel group and has been in effect since 1st March 2024. It is expected that all employees will diligently read and understand this policy and provide their utmost cooperation in its implementation.
