CEO's Office
Sri Lanka Telecom PLC,
Colombo 01.

11th June 2022

To All Staff,

SPECIAL WORK ARRANGEMENTS ON 13th JUNE 2022

Please be informed that 13th June 2022 is considered to be a normal working day and the following special work arrangements will be implemented across SLT PLC.

a. Work From Home (WFH) arrangements will be implemented, where possible subject to the approval of relevant CXO, GMM and DGMM.

b. Home to Field (H2F) arrangements will be implemented to manage all Regional OPMC and Network Operations where deemed necessary.

c. All Regional Telecom Offices (RTOs) and Teleshops (TS) will be open for public to maintain Bill Collection and Customer Service activities. All Regional Management are advised to arrange required staff to man payments and customer service counters in RTO & TS on above day appropriately.

d. All shift operations will be continued in usual manner as per pre-scheduled rosters as a working day.

However, the employees who have already been assigned to operational/essential duties on above day should report for work as usual.

Janaka R Abeysinghe
Chief Executive Officer
