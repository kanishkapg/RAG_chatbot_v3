CEO’s Circular No: 87/2023

CEO’s Office, Sri Lanka Telecom PLC, Colombo 01.
30th November 2023

To All Staff,

Organizational Re-alignment

Considering the operational requirements, following organization re-alignment has been made with immediate effect.

| Organizational Unit | Existing Structure | New Structure |
|---|---|---|
| Complain Resolving Centre (CRC) Unit. | DGM - Operation Support | DGM - Collections & Credit Control. |

Accordingly, CRC responsibilities and the relevant staff currently attached under the DGM-Operation Support shall be transferred to the DGM-Collections & Credit Control.

Janaka R Abeysinghe Chief Executive Officer
