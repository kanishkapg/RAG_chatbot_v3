CEO’s Circular No:33/2023

CEO’s Office, Sri Lanka Telecom PLC, Colombo 01
15th May 2023

To All Staff,

Covering up of Duties – CEO/SLT Digital Services (Pvt) Ltd

Mr. S. M. A. U. S. Manchanayake, CEO of SLT Digital Services (Pvt) Ltd. will be on leave from 13th May 2023.

Accordingly, Mr. W. A. K. D. Weerakkody, GM/T & Digital Services of SLT Digital Services (Pvt) Ltd., has been appointed to cover up the duties of CEO/SLT Digital Services (Pvt) Ltd with effect from 13th May 2023, until Mr. Manchanayake reports back to work.

Janaka R Abeysinghe
Chief Executive Officer
