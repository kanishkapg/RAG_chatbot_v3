CEO's Circular No: O5/2022

CEO's Office, Sri Lanka Telecom PLC, Colombo 01.

To: All SLT Employees,

SALARY INCREASE FOR THE YEAR 2022

SLT Management is pleased to inform you that the Board of Directors has approved the salary increase for the year 2022 in following manner to all SLT permanent staff (executive & non-executive staff) and to the employees who were recruited on contract basis from HCS to SLT (under the intake of June 2020) with effect from 01st January 2022. This salary increase will be subject to the other conditions stipulated in the "Annual Salary Revision, Bonus & Leave Encashment Procedure" of SLT. Employees who have taken unauthorized no pay leave during the year 2021 will not be entitled for the Annual Salary increase.

a. 8.5% salary increase (fixed portion) based on company performance in year 2021 with effect from 1st January 2022. This increase will be added to the basic salary during the month of January 2022.

b. 1.5% salary increase based on the individual performances in line with the criteria detailed below with effect from 1st January 2022. This increase will be added to the basic salary during the month of February 2022 after performance evaluation process.

| Performance Rating | Percentage of Salary Increase (Individual Performance Based Increase) |
|---|---|
| 5    | 1.5%    |
| 4    | 1.2%    |
| 3    | 0.9%    |
| 2    | 0.6%    |
| 1    | 0%    |

The management of SLT is grateful on the initiatives taken by all members of the staff in improving productivity during the last year. We look forward to your fullest support in the future as well.

K. A. Kiththi Perera
Chief Executive Officer
