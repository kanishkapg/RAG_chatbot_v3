SLT management is pleased to acknowledge and recognize your commitment to maintain our services during the year 2024 and our efforts resulted in reducing costs and contributions towards a positive financial result in Q3, 2024.

Considering your contributions during the year 2024 and based on the organization performances, the Board of Directors have approved a total annual bonus for the year 2024 as follows subject to setting off the payment of cash advance paid in April 2024 by SLT PLC.

| Description of Payments    | Payment Scenario    |
|---|---|
| Total Bonus, comprises of the "Fixed Bonus" and "Performance Bonus" | Maximum up to 2 % months of basic salary for the year 2024, subject to a minimum of LKR 150,000.00 per employee. |
| Fixed Bonus    | 1 3/4 months of basic salary for the year 2024 subject to a minimum of LKR 150,000.00 per employee. This payment will be done subject to setting off the payment of cash advance paid in April 2024. |
| Performance based Bonus    | A target performance bonus of three quarter month (3/4) of basic salary based on the individual performance ratings for the year 2024. This payment will be done subsequent to the performance evaluation process. |

Table 01: Payment of Annual Bonus for the year 2024

General Conditions:

I. SLT Permanent Employees recruited before the year 2024 including the employees who were recruited on a contract basis from HCS to SLT (under the intake of June 2020)

Eligible for the fixed bonus and performance based bonus for the year 2024 as detailed in the above table 01.

II. SLT Permanent Employees recruited during the year 2024.

a. Employees who joined SLT between 01.01.2024 - 30.06.2024 will be eligible for the fixed bonus and performance based bonus for the year 2024 as detailed in the above table 01 on prorate basis.

b. Employees who joined SLT between 01.07.2024 - 30.09.2024 will be eligible only for the fixed bonus for the year 2024 as detailed in the above table 01 on prorate basis. Accordingly, the said employees are not entitled to the performance based bonus for the year 2024.

c. Employees who joined SLT after 01.10.2024 are not entitled to the bonus payments for the year 2024.

III. Contract Basis Employees

a. A payment of festival allowance will be made following the similar criteria of payment of bonus to SLT permanent employees as detailed under the section I & II above.

All Bonus payments will be made in accordance with the Bonus Payment Procedure of the company.

I take this opportunity to thank all of you for your significant contribution and dedication during this year. The year ahead will bring its own challenges & opportunities and working as a one team will pave the way to realize corporate objectives of the organization in the year 2025.

I wish all SLT employees and members of their families a Merry Christmas and a Prosperous New Year.
