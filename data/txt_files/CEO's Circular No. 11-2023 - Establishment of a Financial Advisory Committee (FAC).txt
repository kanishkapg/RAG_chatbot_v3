Establishment of a Financial Advisory Committee (FAC)

SLT management is pleased to inform the staff that the management has taken steps to establish a Financial Advisory Committee (FAC) to act as the financial planning partner of the staff. The Financial Advisory Committee shall make the staff aware and help the staff to realize their future goals. The awareness process may involve detailed advisory help with financial topics such as investment advice, debt management, budgeting, retirement planning, insurance, and tax matters etc.

As the initial step the Financial Advisory Committee shall understand the employee’s financial health, where the employee stands today, by working with the employee to get a complete picture of the employee’s assets liabilities, income, and expenses. The next step will be to combine all the initial information into a comprehensive financial plan that will serve as a roadmap for employee financial future. This financial plan will recap the goals that the Financial Advisory Committee and the employee discussed.

3. Responsibilities of FAC

The responsibilities of FAC are set out below. The order of the items reflects no particular order of importance or priority.

- Discussing with employees to determine their expenses, income, insurance coverage, financial objectives, tax status, risk tolerance, or other information needed to develop a financial plan.
- Answering employee questions about financial plans and strategies and giving financial advice.
- Advising strategies for employees in insurance coverage, investment planning, cash management, and other areas to help them reach financial objectives.

4. FAC Meetings

FAC shall meet as often as deemed necessary, to comply with its responsibilities as set forth herein. FAC may request the employee concern, to attend a meeting of FAC to provide more clarification on the information provided by them. FAC shall record its important deliberations for future reference, in terms of the issues discussed, and the conclusions arrived at, with the minutes signed by all the members of FAC.

5. Liability Disclaimer

Information disclosed to FAC by the employees shall be treated as Strictly Confidential.

Notwithstanding anything to the contrary hereof, in no event shall the FAC be liable for any damage/loss caused to an employee by acting upon the advice of the FAC.
