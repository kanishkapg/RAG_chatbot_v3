CEO's Circular No: 23/2024

CEO's Office, Sri Lanka Telecom PLC, Colombo 01.
20th February 2024

To: All SLT Employees,

SALARY INCREASE FOR THE YEAR 2024

SLT Management is pleased to inform you that the Board of Directors has approved a salary increase of up to a maximum value of 6% for the year 2024. The salary increase for 2024 is comprised of two components as shown below to all SLT permanent staff (executive and non-executive staff) and to the employees who were recruited on contract basis from HCS to SLT (under the intake of June 2020). The fixed component of the increment will be paid in the month of February and the performance component will be added subsequently in the month of March. Both the fixed component and the performance component of the salary increase will be effective as of 01st January 2024 subject to the other conditions stipulated in the "Annual Salary Revision, Bonus & Leave Encashment Procedure" of SLT. However, employees who have taken unauthorized no pay leave during the year 2023 will not be entitled for the Annual Salary increase.

a. 5% Salary Increase (Fixed portion).

b. Up to 1% Salary Increase based on the individual performances in line with the criteria detailed below.

| Performance Rating | Percentage of Individual Performance Based Salary Increase |
|--------------------|-----------------------------------------------------------|
| 5                  | 1 %                                                       |
| 4                  | 0.8 %                                                     |
| 3                  | 0.6 %                                                     |
| 2                  | 0.4 %                                                     |
| 1                  | 0 %                                                       |

The management of SLT looks forward to your continued support in improving productivity, driving company goals and achieving a profitable business in 2024.
