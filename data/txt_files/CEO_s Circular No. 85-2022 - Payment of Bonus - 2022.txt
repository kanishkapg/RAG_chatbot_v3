CEO's Circular No. 85 / 2022

CEO's Office, Sri Lanka Telecom PLC, Colombo 01.

30th November 2022

To: All SLT Employees,

PAYMENT OF BONUS - 2022

SLT management is pleased to acknowledge and recognize your commitment to maintain our services during the year 2022.

Considering your contributions during the year 2022 and based on the organization performances, the Board of Directors have approved a total annual bonus for the year 2022 as follows subject to setting off the payment of cash advance paid in April 2022 by SLT PLC.

| Description of Payments | Payment Scenario |
|---|---|
| Total Bonus    | Maximum up to 3 ½ months of basic salary for the year 2022. |
| Fixed Bonus    | 03 months of basic salary for the year 2022 subject to a minimum of LKR 150,000/- per employee. This payment will be paid on 30th November 2022 subject to setting off the payment of cash advance paid in April 2022. |
| Performance based Bonus    | Up to half a month (½) of basic salary based on the individual performance ratings for the year 2022. This payment will be paid in January 2023 subsequent to the performance evaluation process. |

Table 01: Payment of Annual Bonus for the year 2022

General Conditions:

I. SLT Permanent Employees recruited before the year 2022 including the employees who were recruited on contract basis from HCS to SLT (under the intake of June 2020)

Eligible for the fixed bonus and performance based bonus for the year 2022 as detailed in the above table 01.

Page 1 of 2

II. SLT Permanent Employees recruited during the year 2022.

a. Employees who joined SLT between 01.01.2022 - 30.06.2022 will be eligible for the fixed bonus and performance based bonus for the year 2022 as detailed in the above table 01 on prorate basis.

b. Employees who joined SLT between 01.07.2022 - 30.09.2022 will be eligible only for the fixed bonus for the year 2022 as detailed in the above table 01 on prorate basis. Accordingly said employees are not entitled for the performance based bonus for the year 2022.

c. Employees who joined SLT after 01.10.2022 are not entitled to the bonus payments for the year 2022.

III. Contract Basis Employees

a. A payment of festival allowance will be made following the similar criteria of payment of bonus to SLT permanent employees as detailed under the section I & II above.

All Bonus payments will be made in accordance with the Bonus Payment Procedure of the company.

I take this opportunity to thank all of you for your significant contribution and dedication during this year. The year ahead will bring its own challenges & opportunities and working as a one team will pave the way to realize corporate objectives of the organization in the year 2023.

I wish all SLT employees and members of their families a happy and a prosperous New Year.

Janaka R Abeysinghe
Chief Executive Officer
