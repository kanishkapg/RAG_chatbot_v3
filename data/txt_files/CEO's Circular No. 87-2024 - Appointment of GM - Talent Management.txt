CEO’s Office, Sri Lanka Telecom PLC, Colombo 01

To All Staff,

APPOINTMENT OF GENERAL MANAGER-TALENT MANAGEMENT

Please be informed that Ms. Thushari Rambukpotha has been appointed as the General Manager-Talent Management of SLTMobitel with immediate effect.

Ms. Rambukpotha will report to the Chief People Officer SLTMobitel.

Ms. Rambukpotha is a professional with nearly 20 years of extensive experience in HR, Project Management and Marketing. Prior to joining SLT, she has worked as the Assistant Director/HR & Organization Transformation - Securities & Exchange Commission of Sri Lanka, Deputy General Manager / Group Performance Management & Succession Planning - Hela Clothing, Project Manager- Kaley Natural Farms, Manager /Operations - Brandix Lanka, Manager /Human Resources - Brandix Essentials, Sri Lanka, Senior Executive /Marketing - Brandix Essentials, Senior Executive /Project Management - Brandix India Apparel City (BIAC) – Sri Lanka & India.

She holds a Masters in Human Resource Management from York St. John University-UK, a Bachelor of Science in Marketing from the University of Sri Jayewardenepura and an Advance Certificate in Marketing – CIM (UK).

We welcome Ms. Thushari Rambukpotha to SLTMobitel and wish her all success in her new job role.

Janaka R Abeysinghe
Chief Executive Officer
