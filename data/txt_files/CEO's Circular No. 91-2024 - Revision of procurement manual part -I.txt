Revision of Procurement Manual Part-I

SLT Procurement manual part-I has been revised to incorporate the board approved Policy for Placing Suppliers/Vendors/any other party on a "Non-Compliant Suppliers/Vendors List", which accommodates the necessary improvements in accordance with the best practices and the standards of procurements to address the present and the future business needs of the company.

Revised Procurement Manual Part-I shall be effective from 30th October 2024 and is available in SLT common documents portal as mentioned below for your reference.

*Intranet → General → SLT Common Documents Portal → Documents → BusinessGuides → Procurement Manual*

The procurement manual part I includes the general parameters of procurements based on the governing set of principles & policies to manage the full cycle of the acquisition of goods/services from the requirement identification up to the completion of delivery to the company complying with the agreed terms and conditions.

Accordingly, all employees are required to comply with the principles, policies, and guidelines of the revised procurement manual Part I with effect from 30th October 2024.

Janaka R. Abeysinghe
