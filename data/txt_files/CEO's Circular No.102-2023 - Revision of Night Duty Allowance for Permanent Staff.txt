# Revision of Night Duty Allowance for Permanent Staff

Night Duty allowance will be increased with effect from 01st January 2024 as below;

|    | Category I (Employees in salary Grade “B” and “C”) | Category II (Employees in salary Grade “D”) | Category III (Employees in salary Grade “S”) |
|---|---|---|---|
| Per night rate   | Rs. 600 per day    | Rs. 300 per day    | Rs. 600 per day    |

Other conditions of paying night duty allowance will remain unchanged.
