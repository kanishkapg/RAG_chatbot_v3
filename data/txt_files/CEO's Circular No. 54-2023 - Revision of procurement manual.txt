Revision of Procurement Manual Part I

Board of Directors has approved the revised SLT Procurement manual part-I which accommodates the necessary improvements in accordance with the best practices and the standards of procurements to address the present and the future business needs of the company.

Revised Procurement Manual shall be effective from 03rd August 2023 and is available in SLT common documents portal as mentioned below for your reference.

*Intranet → General → SLT Common Documents Portal → Links → Guidelines, Manuals and Handbooks → Procurement Manual*

The procurement manual part 1 includes the general parameters of procurements based on the governing set of principles & policies to manage the full cycle of the acquisition of goods/services from the requirement identification up to the completion of delivery to the company complying with the agreed terms and conditions.

Accordingly, all employees are required to comply with the principles, policies and guidelines of the revised procurement manual with effect from 03rd August 2023.

Janaka R. Abeysinghe
Chief Executive Officer
