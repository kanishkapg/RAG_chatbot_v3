To: All General Managers & Deputy General Managers,

POST OF GENERAL MANAGER â€“ TALENT MANAGEMENT

Applications are invited from the permanent General Managers/ Deputy General Managers who satisfy the under mentioned requirements to be considered for the above post.

1. ELIGIBILITY FOR TRANSFER

GMs (A1, A2 & A3) who wish to be transferred to above position shall apply.

2. ELIGIBILITY FOR PROMOTION

2.1 FOR DGMs PRESENTLY IN A2 GRADE:

DGMs (A2) who wish to be considered for the above position shall apply.

2.2 FOR DGMs PRESENTLY IN A3 GRADE:

(a) Engineering Profession

(i) Corporate Membership of IESL/IET (IEE) UK/IMechE (UK)/Institution of Civil Engineers (UK) with 5 years' experience in A3 Grade in SLT.

(b) Accounting Profession

(i) Associate Membership of ICA/CIMA/ACCA with 5 years' experience in A3 Grade in SLT

(c) Human Resources Profession

(i) Degree of a recognized university with full membership of CIPM (IPM) Sri Lanka with 5 years' experience in A3 Grade in SLT

(d) Marketing Profession

(i) MCIM(UK) with Chartered Marketer status with 5 years' experience in A3 Grade in SLT

(e) Legal Profession

(i) An Attorney at law and Notary Public with 5 years' experience in A3 Grade in SLT

2.3 FOR DGMs PRESENTLY IN A4 GRADE:

(a) Engineering Profession
(i) Corporate Membership of IESL/IET (IEE) UK/ IMechE (UK)/Institution of Civil Engineers (UK) with 3 years engineering experience in A4 Grade in SLT.
OR
(ii) An Engineering Degree/Associate Membership of IESL/IET (IEE) UK/IMechE (UK)/Institution of Civil Engineers (UK) with 5 years engineering experience in A4 Grade in SLT.
OR
(iii) 7 years engineering experience in A4 Grade in SLT

(b) Accounting Profession
(i) Associate Membership of ICA/CIMA/ACCA with 3 years accounting / auditing experience in A4 Grade in SLT
OR
(ii) Intermediate qualification of ICA/CIMA/ACCA with 5 years accounting / auditing experience in A4 Grade in SLT
OR
(iii) 7 years accounting / auditing experience in A4 Grade in SLT

(c) Human Resources Profession
(i) Degree of a recognized university with full membership of CIPM (IPM) Sri Lanka with 3 years' experience in A4 Grade in SLT
OR
ii) A Degree of a recognized university or associate membership of CIPM (IPM) with 5 years' experience in A4 Grade in SLT
OR
(ii) 7 years' experience in A4 Grade in SLT

(d) Marketing Profession
(i) MCIM(UK) with Chartered Marketer status with 3 years' experience in A4 Grade in SLT
OR
(ii) MCIM(UK) with 5 years' experience in marketing in A4 Grade in SLT
OR
(iii) 7 years marketing experience in A4 Grade in SLT

(e) Legal Profession
(i) An Attorney at law and Notary Public with 5 years' experience in A4 Grade in SLT

An MBA in Business Management / HRM from a recognized university will be an added qualification

2.4 Deputy General Managers in A3 Grade who do not possess required number of years' experience in A3 Grade under category 2.2 will be eligible under category 2.3 by adding the number of years' experience in A4 Grade and A3 Grade together and those officers may also apply.

3 SELECTION METHOD
Selection will be done by an interview.

4 PLACEMENT

(a) Transfer (As per Section 1)
Selected General Manager will be transferred as GM- Talent Management

(b) Promotion (As per Section 2)
If the selected officer is in A3 Grade, he/she will be placed in A2 Grade and if the selected officer is in A4 Grade he/she will be placed in A3 Grade subject to the provisions available in the SRPS. Further, if the selected officer is in A2 Grade, his/her Grade will remain unchanged.

5 METHOD OF APPLYING
Eligible candidates should fill in the attached application form and forward to DGM-HR Operations through the relevant Supervising Officer.

6 CLOSING DATE
Applications should be sent within 07 working days of issuing this Circular.

Janaka R Abeysinghe
Chief Executive Officer
