Revision of SLT Health & Safety Policy, Health & Safety Risk Management Process Document and introduction of new procedures in support of established Health & Safety Management System

The SLT Health & Safety Policy and the Health & Safety Risk Management Process have been revised to suit the recent changes in the organization, and new procedures have been introduced in support of the established Health & Safety Management System. The listed documents will be effective from 22nd January 2024.
