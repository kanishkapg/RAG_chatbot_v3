Organizational Re-arrangement – Regional Business

RTO- Embilipitiya and RTO- Mullaitivu have been declared as Teleshops with effect from 1st of March 2023 by considering the business requirements.

| Existing Structure | New Structure |
|---|---|
|    | Teleshop Name    | RTO Attached    |
| RTO-Embilipitiya    | Teleshop- Embilipitiya    | RTO-Hambantota    |
| RTO-Mullaitivu    | Teleshop- Mullaitivu    | RTO-Kilinochchi    |

Janaka R Abeysinghe
