CEO's Circular No: 39/2024

CEO's Office, Sri Lanka Telecom PLC, Colombo 01.
24 April 2024

To All Security Staff,

PROMOTION TO THE RANKS OF SECURITY OFFICER, PATROL LEADER AND TEAM LEADER (SECURITY)

Applications are invited from the permanent, confirmed SLT Security Staff who satisfy the under mentioned requirements to be considered for the above mentioned positions.

A. ELIGIBILITY

Team Leader (Security) - S4

• 10 years satisfactory service as a Patrolman in the S5 salary grade at SLT as at 1st April 2024
AND
Should have followed at least 05 prescribed training courses conducted by SLT Security Division whilst service as a Patrolman

Patrol Leader - S3

• 10 years confirmed & satisfactory service in SLT with minimum 01 year service in S4 salary grade as a Team Leader (Security) as at 1st April 2024
AND
Should have followed at least 03 prescribed training courses conducted by SLT Security Division whilst service as a Team Leader (Security)

Security Officer – S2 (Southern & Sabaragamuwa / North & East / NWP,CP & Uva)

• 10 years confirmed & satisfactory service in SLT with minimum 01 year service in S3 salary grade as a Patrol Leader as at 1st April 2024
AND
• Should have followed at least 03 prescribed training courses conducted by SLT Security Division whilst serving as a Patrol Leader

B. SELECTION METHOD

Selection will be made through an interview.

C. PLACEMENT

Selected candidates will be promoted as Security Officer S2 /Team Leader (Security) - S4 / Patrol Leader – S3 and placed under vacant positions whilst Security Officers will be placed as Security Officer – Southern & Sabaragamuwa / Security Officer – North & East / Security Officer – NWP, CP & Uva)

D. METHOD OF APPLYING

Eligible candidates should fill in the attached application form and forward to DGM-HR Operations 7th Floor, HQ Building, Sri Lanka Telecom PLC, Lotus Road, Colombo 01 through the relevant General Manager / Deputy General Manager.

E. CLOSING DATE

Applications should be sent within seven (07) working days of issuing this circular. Applications sent after the closing date will not be considered.

Janaka R Abeysinghe
Chief Executive Officer
