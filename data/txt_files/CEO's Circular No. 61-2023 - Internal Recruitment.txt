CEO’s Circular No: 61/2023

CEO’s Office, Sri Lanka Telecom PLC, Colombo 01.

23rd August 2023

To All SLT Staff,

Internal Recruitment

Applications are invited from permanent SLT employees who possess following qualifications and fulfill other eligibility criteria as at 31 July 2023 for the posts stated below.

- Engineer-A5.
- Telecommunication Technical Officer-A9.
- IT & Network Officer-A9/Software Developer-A9.

1. Qualifications & Eligibility Criteria for the post of Engineer-A5:

a) Four-year Degree in BSc Engineering/ Bachelor of Technology (Telecommunication/Electrical & Electronic/ Computer Engineering/ Information Engineering) from a University/Institute recognized by UGC and Institute of Engineers, Sri Lanka, equivalent to SLQF 6.
OR
b) Associate Member of the Institution of Engineers, Sri Lanka.
AND
c) Three years of permanent confirmed and satisfactory service in SLT as at the date specified above.

2. Qualifications & Eligibility Criteria for the post of Telecommunication Technical Officer-A9:

a) G.C.E. (O.L) examination with six passes at one sitting with credit passes in Sinhala/Tamil, English Language and Mathematics.
AND
b) A Higher Diploma in Telecommunication/Electronic/Communication Technology/Computer Technology/ Electrical/Digital Technology/Power/Air Conditioning, etc. equivalent to National Vocational Qualification 6 (NVQ-6) or SLQF 4 or above from a University/Institute recognized by UGC Sri Lanka or any other government authorities.
OR
c) Registration at Engineering Council, Sri Lanka as an “Engineering Diplomate” in relevant field.
AND
d) Three years of permanent, confirmed and satisfactory service in SLT as at the date specified above.

3. Qualifications & Eligibility Criteria for the post of IT & Network Officer-A9/Software Developer-A9:

a) G.C.E. (O/L) Examination with 06 passes at one sitting with credit passes for Sinhala/Tamil, English Language and Mathematics.

AND
b) ACS Diploma, BCS Diploma, Higher Diploma in System Design, IT, Computer Science, Computer Engineering, Software Engineering, Software Development, Web Designing etc obtained from a recognized institute by the UGC-Sri Lanka or any other government authorities, equivalent to NVQ-6 or SLQF 4 or above.
OR
c) BTEC (IT stream) qualification and 02 years post qualified experience at SLT.
AND
d) Three years of permanent, confirmed and satisfactory service in SLT as at the date specified above.

4. Method of Selection:

Selection will be made through a written examination (English Medium). Only those who achieve required competency at the examination will be called for an interview for final selection. Placement will be done on par with organizational operational exigency.

5. Method of Applying:

Application should be submitted through ERP System to reach HR Strategies & Policy Section within 10 days of issuing this circular. Scan copies of relevant certificates should be attached.

ERP System>SLT Employee Self-Service>Other Requests>Internal Recruitments>Click on 'Create Application'>Select the Post Applied> Attach Relevant Certificates>Add Qualifications>Click on 'Create'>Click on 'Application Number'>tick the 'Declaration' >Submit. (Application will be sent to A5 or above supervisor for recommendation and to the HR thereafter).

Applications sent without supporting documents will not be entertained. Only shortlisted applicants will be contacted by HR.

Janaka R Abeysinghe
Chief Executive Officer
