CEO’s Circular No: 86 / 2023

CEO’s Office,
Sri Lanka Telecom PLC,
Colombo 01

23rd November 2023

**To All Staff,**

**MAINTAINING UNINTERRUPTED OPERATIONS & CUSTOMER SERVICES ON**
**24TH NOVEMBER 2023**

SLT’s operation is essential for the continuation of critical services and business activities of our valued
customers. Therefore, it is the responsibility of all employees to discharge their assigned business/operational
duties with no disturbance to customer services. We all are aware that any interruption in our operations will
adversely affect the image of the company’s business.

The management has already taken all the measures to continue the business and operations of the company in
the usual manner. Further, all the Chief Officers, the Regional, Divisional and the Sectional management
**are empowered to take the business and operational decisions to ensure business continuity of the**
**company.**

Employees who have not reported for duty citing sickness, personal reasons, or any other reasons, are not
allowed to enter the SLT premises on 24[th] November 2023. Employees are instructed not to disrupt any SLT
services/operations by staying in front of SLT office premises, and not to indulge in any activities that can
tarnish the brand image of SLT. Employees are also instructed not to block any entrances to the SLT premises
that may lead to obstructing the employees, customers, suppliers from entering SLT offices. Further, the
employees shall not engage in any forceful intervention, or threaten other employees who attend to their
normal duties.

**The management seeks cooperation of all staff to refrain from engaging in any activity which is**
**detrimental to the interests of the company and to report to their workstations and engage in the**
**assigned duties as directed by their immediate supervisors to maintain uninterrupted operations and**
**customer services.**

Employees are hereby strongly advised not to violate any company rules and procedures, and management will
be compelled to take disciplinary actions against employees for any such violations.

**Janaka R Abeysinghe**
**Chief Executive Officer**
