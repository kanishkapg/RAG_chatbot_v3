CEO's Office, Sri Lanka Telecom PLC, Colombo 01.

To: All Staff,

APPOINTMENT OF GENERAL MANAGER – COMMERCIAL AND COST OPTIMIZATION

Please be informed that Mr. Saliya Jayaweera has been appointed as the General Manager – Commercial and Cost Optimization of SLTMobitel Group with effect from 1st February 2024.

Mr. Jayaweera will report to the Chief Executive Officer.

Mr. Jayaweera is a senior finance professional with over 3 decades of extensive experience in financial services industry with cross culture transformational leadership in multiple markets in Asia, Middle East and Europe. He brings a wealth of experience from multiple business sectors and strong acumen of analytics, problem solving, project management, system implementation, mergers and acquisitions.

Prior to joining SLT, he held senior positions as Management Advisory - Advisory Services in Sri Lanka / KSA, Financial Controller – Banque Saudi Fransi in KSA, Partner – KPMG in Maldives, Senior Director - StratisBridge Advisors in Middle East, Group Head of Finance - EAP Holdings in Sri Lanka, AVP Group Business Performance / Manager Group Integration / Group Management Accountant / Head of Finance-Consumer Bank in Emirates NBD (Emirates Bank) in UAE, Manager / Group ALM Process & Overseas Finance - Mashreq Bank in UAE, Financial Controller - Standard Chartered Bank in UAE & Qatar and Finance Manager - Standard Chartered Bank in Sri Lanka & UK.

He is a Fellow member of the Institute of Chartered Accountants of Sri Lanka.

We welcome Mr. Saliya Jayaweera to SLTMobitel Group and wish him all success in his new job role.

Janaka R Abeysinghe
Chief Executive Officer
