Discontinuation of Technical and Commercial Evaluation Teams (TCET)

With the revision of SLT Procurement Manual Part-I, Technical and Commercial Evaluation Teams (TCET) established by the CEO's circular 12/2021 dated 02nd March 2021 is abolished with effect from 04th April 2022.

Technical Evaluation Teams (TET), Commercial Evaluation Teams (CET), Special Commercial Evaluation Teams (SCET), Sole Source Evaluation Teams (SSET) and Enterprise Solutions TCET (ES-TCET) shall continue to function as set out in the revised procurement manual part-I.

All the details relevant to evaluation teams are given in procurement manual part-I and it could be accessed through SLT Intranet as follows.

Intranet → SLT Common Documents Portal → Links → Guidelines, Manuals and Handbooks → Procurement Manual

All are required to comply with the above.

Janaka R Abeysinghe
Chief Executive Officer
