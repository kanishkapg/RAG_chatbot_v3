REVISION OF RETIREMENT AGE OF SLT EMPLOYEES UNDER THE SERVICE EXTENSION PROCEDURE

Please be informed that as per the provisions of the Minimum Retirement Age of Workers Act No 28 of 2021, the following criteria is implemented with effect from 17th November 2021 with respect to the service extensions/retirements of permanent employees of SLT PLC.

1. All permanent SLT employees are allowed to be in service until the compulsory age of retirement of 60 years without a request for service extension after reaching the age of 55 years subject to items 2, 3 and 4 below.

2. However the services of an employee could be ended before reaching the minimum age of retirement due to permanent incapacity, as a result of disciplinary action, closure of the establishment due to natural causes, under the provisions of the Termination of Employment of Workmen (Special Provisions) Act, No. 45 of 1971 as amended, by resignation etc. as stipulated in "Minimum Retirement Age of Workers Act No 28 of 2021" and/or due to the reasons as provided under Law.

3. The employees aged 52 to 55 years as at 17th November 2021 can retire from the service of SLT PLC as desired by the employee at the minimum age of retirement stipulated under the Minimum Retirement Age of Workers Act No 28 of 2021 and up to the Compulsory Age of Retirement by providing three (03) months prior notice to DGM/HR Operations.

4. Any employee who is at the age of 55 or above as at the date of the 17th November 2021 can retire from the service of SLT PLC as desired by the employee by providing three (03) months prior notice to DGM/HR Operations.

Compulsory Age of Retirement for all permanent employees of SLT PLC is Sixty (60) years.

The Service Extension Procedure of SLT PLC (Doc No: HSPS/SEP/018) is amended to reflect above detailed provisions and made available via intranet (General>SLT Common Document Portal>Policies>Human Resources<CPO>Service Extension Procedure).

Above Procedure will supersede all circulars, procedures hereto issued in respect of service extensions applicable for all SLT PLC staff.

Janaka R. Abeysinghe Chief Executive Officer
