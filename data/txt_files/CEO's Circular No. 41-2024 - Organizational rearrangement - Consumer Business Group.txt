Organizational Rearrangement - Consumer Business Group

To enhance the focus on retaining existing customers, a new unit called Customer Retention Unit (CRU) is established with immediate effect. CRU will operate directly under the purview of each General Manager (Regional Business), as outlined in Annexure 1.

Every CRU, comprising of three Managers, is tasked with reducing customer churn, addressing early terminations, and reactivating deleted customers. The specific responsibilities and key performance indicators (KPIs) for each manager are detailed in Annexure 2.

This organizational structure of Consumer Business group will be established with immediate effect.

Janaka R. Abeysinghe
Chief Executive Officer
