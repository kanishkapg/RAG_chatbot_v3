To All Staff,

Transfers for SLT Employees – 2025

Transfer applications for the year 2025 are invited from eligible Non-Executive Staff (A16 – A7) and Security Staff (S5 – S2) in accordance with the currently approved SLT Transfer Procedure.

Staff in combined categories (Customer Care Officers, Customer Relations Officers and Senior Assistant Manager (IT) who perform Customer Care job role) will be considered together for the Transfer Scheme 2025.

The principles of sustaining operational continuity, maintaining fairness, equity, employee competency and performance are considered throughout the transfer process to ensure its smooth and transparent implementation. Transfer Application process is automated via ERP and all staff shall use ERP based Transfer application form to submit Transfer requests for the year 2025. Transfer Application Closing date will be 9th September 2024 and applications received after the closing date will not be entertained.
