CEO's Office, Sri Lanka Telecom PLC, Colombo 01.

To All Staff,

ORGANIZATIONAL RE-ALIGNMENT

In order to ensure that the HR group is closely aligned with the overall strategic objectives of the organization, following changes have been made to the SLTMobitel HR Structure with immediate effect. These changes will involve realigning HR functions, roles, and responsibilities to better support the achievement of the organizational objectives.

Accordingly, following new positions are created in the structure,

| Position                            | Reporting to        |
|-------------------------------------|---------------------|
| General Manager - Talent Management | CPO                 |
| Deputy General Manager - Workforce Planning & Analytics | CPO                 |
| Deputy General Manager - Talent Acquisition | DCPO/GM-HCM         |

Positions of General Manager-Employee Experience Management, Health & Safety and Deputy General Manager- HR Strategies & Policy will be abolished. Health & Safety functions will be assigned under DGM-Employee & Industrial Relations.

Further, internal rearrangement within the SLT HR Division will be done to ensure the HR practices and processes are aligned with the new structure.

HR Group's new organization structure is attached as annexure 1.

Janaka R Abeysinghe
Chief Executive Officer
