INTRODUCTION OF CONTRACT EMPLOYMENT PROCEDURE

Contract employment procedure (Document No: HSPS/CEP/021) allows SLT to bridge the talent requirement by hiring highly skilled employees in a specific field for a specific project or for a temporary vacancy lasting for a limited period of time for which their expertise is vital. Moreover, procedure addresses the requirements for specialization and strengthening the Company's business continuation.

The Contract Employment Procedure of SLT PLC (Document No: HSPS/CEP/021) is available at SLT intranet (General>SLT Common Document Portal>Policies>Human Resources (CPO)> Contract Employment Procedure).

Above Procedure will supersede all previous circulars, procedures, approvals and memos hereto issued in this regard.

Janaka R. Abeysinghe
