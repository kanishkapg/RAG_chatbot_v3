CEO’s Circular No: 35 / 2023

CEO’s Office, Sri Lanka Telecom PLC, Colombo 01
18th May 2023

To All Staff,

APPOINTMENT OF GROUP CHIEF OFFICER – CONSUMER BUSINESS

Please be informed that Mr. I B Wijekoon has been appointed as the Group Chief Officer – Consumer Business (GCO-CB) under the SLTMOBITEL unified group organization structure with immediate effect.

Mr. Wijekoon will report to Chief Executive Officer of Sri Lanka Telecom PLC. For Consumer Business related functions associated with Mobitel, he will functionally report to Chief Operating Officer of Mobitel.

Mr. Wijekoon joined SLT in 1994 and in his present role as Chief Regional Business Officer leads the Consumer business of SLT. In his carrier spanning over 28 years, Mr. Wijekoon has held number of senior positions including, Chief Regional Sales Officer, General Manager - Metro Region, Head of Division - Customer Service Centers, Deputy Head of Region - Commercial (Metro), Head of Province – Uva. He has extensive experience in the areas of Consumer and SME business, Customer Experience management, Project Lead Management and Field Service Operations.

He holds an MBA from the University of Sri Jayewardenepura and a BSc degree in Electronics and Telecommunications Engineering from the University of Moratuwa. Mr. Wijekoon is a Chartered Engineer, a member of the Institution of Engineers Sri Lanka and functions as a Non-Executive Board Director of SLT Services Pvt. Ltd since 2019.

We wish him all success in his new job role.

Janaka R Abeysinghe
Chief Executive Officer
