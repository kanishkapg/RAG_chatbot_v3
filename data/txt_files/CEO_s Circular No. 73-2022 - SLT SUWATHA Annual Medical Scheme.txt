SLT "SUWATHA" ANNUAL MEDICAL SCHEME

SLT management has made all arrangements to conduct the Suwatha Annual Medical Scheme for all permanent employees of SLT from 01.10.2022 to 28.02.2023.

All permanent employees are hereby informed to undergo the Medical Tests that they are entitled for, based on the age, during the stipulated period. Requests to extend the period will not be entertained.

Employees should submit the requests for Medical Tests at least 03 working days prior to the date that they plan to undergo the Medical Tests using the following link.

Suwatha Application Link: : https://employeerelations.slt.com.lk/

The employees themselves can verify the data entered via the Suwatha Application Management System.

The supervisors are responsible to inform about the Suwatha Annual Medical Scheme to their subordinates who do not have access to e-mails. All the leave agents are requested to support the employees who do not have access to computers to enter their details.

Suwatha Application User Guide can be accessed through the following path.

Intranet → e-application → ER section → Suwatha user guide

Once an employee makes a request to undergo the Medical Tests at a particular hospital, the medical section will forward the following information to the hospital.

1. Details of the said employee.
2. The date requested to conduct Medical Tests.
3. The tests that the employee will undergo.

The employee has to produce the SLT Identity Card at the Suwatha Registration Desk at the hospital to prove his/her identity. On completion of all the entitled Medical Tests, the employee should place the signature on the "list of employees" confirming the completion of all tests.

Annexure I

Suwatha Annual Medical Scheme

Instructions

1. You should be fasting for 12-14 consecutive hours to do the Lipid Profile Test. The hospital commences performing tests from 8.00 a.m.; accordingly, you have to calculate the hours to start fasting.

2. After doing Lipid Profile, employees who undergo Ultrasound Scan should not have food, but need to drink water or King Coconut to do the Ultrasound Scan.

3. Make sure to take your spectacles and SLT ID card along with you.

4. Since the hospital has to get down specialists for medical tests such as 2D Echo & US Scan in abdomen, employees need to reserve the date at least 3 working days in advance.

5. By accessing the Suwatha Application Management System, you can see whether your request is confirmed. You will receive a SMS confirmation to the mobile number entered to the Suwatha Application Management System, once your information is transferred to the hospital.

6. These tests under the same subsidized rates can be done for immediate family members and retired employees by paying directly to the hospital on the day of the tests.

7. Mammogram test will not be conducted by the Singha Hospital Rathnapura and Melsta Hospital (Pvt) Ltd and employees who have opted to do the Mammogram Test should select any other Hospital.

8. If you have participated for Suwatha Annual Medical Scheme last year, you should fill the feedback form before applying for this year Suwatha Program.
