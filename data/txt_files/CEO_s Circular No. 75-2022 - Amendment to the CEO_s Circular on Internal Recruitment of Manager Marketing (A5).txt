CEO's Circular No: 75/2022

CEO's Office, Sri Lanka Telecom PLC, Colombo 01.

11th October 2022

To All SLT Staff,

Amendment to the CEO's Circular on Internal Recruitment of Manager Marketing (A5)

Eligibility Criteria & Qualifications for the above recruitment stated under the section 01 of the CEO's Circular No 61/2022 dated 17th August 2022 will be amended as follows.

Special Degree in Marketing  
OR  
Postgraduate Diploma in Marketing from SLIM.  
OR  
Professional Postgraduate Diploma in Marketing from CIM-UK.  
AND  
Two years post qualifying experience in Marketing/Sales in SLT as at 31st July 2022.

Other conditions stated in the above circular shall remain unchanged. Only new applicants should submit application through ERP System to reach HR Strategies & Policy Section within 07 days of issuing this circular. Scan copies of relevant certificates should be attached.

Janaka R. Abeysinghe  
Chief Executive Officer
