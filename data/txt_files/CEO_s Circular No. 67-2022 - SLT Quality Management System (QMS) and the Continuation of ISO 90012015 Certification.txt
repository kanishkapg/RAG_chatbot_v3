SLT Quality Management System (QMS) and the Continuation of ISO 9001:2015 Certification

SLT has initiated the ISO 9001:2015 QMS re-certification program with the aim of further improving the overall quality of SLT services.

The main objective of SLT's QMS is predominantly to ensure the highest quality in customer services by way of compliance to standards, focus on risk management and continual improvements of the activities. International Organization for Standardization has introduced ISO 9001:2015 standard to help organizations to achieve such objectives by adhering to its guidelines on company processes and procedures in practice.

It is the responsibility of all employees to maintain the SLT QMS by giving due consideration to comply given guidelines on processes and procedures in daily management and operational activities appropriately, thereby achieving the company objective; ISO 9001:2015 re-certified status for SLT.

Please be noted that "SLT Quality Policy" and the "SLT QMS Scope" have been revised. Updated documents and other related guidelines are published at:

SLT Intranet ->IS & QM Division ->QM Section ->SLT QMS Documents.
