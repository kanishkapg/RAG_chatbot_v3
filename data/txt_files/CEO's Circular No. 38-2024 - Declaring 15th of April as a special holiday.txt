To All Staff,

DECLARING 15TH OF APRIL 2024 AS A SPECIAL HOLIDAY FOR SLT STAFF

SLT Management has decided to declare Monday, the 15th of April 2024 as a special Holiday for all SLT employees.

However, the employees who have already been assigned operational/essential duties on the 15th of April 2024, shall report to work and the relevant payments due to them will be made accordingly.

Janaka R Abeysinghe
Chief Executive Officer
