CEO's Circular No. 91/2022

CEO's Office, Sri Lanka Telecom PLC, Colombo 01.

16th December 2022

To: All Employees,

Declaration of SLT Annual Big Cleaning Day -2022

"Productivity Improvement through Clean & Organized Workplace"

I hereby declare 22nd December 2022 as "SLT Annual Big Cleaning Day - 2022" to carry out 5S activities.

A pleasant, safe, healthy, and productive work environment and a new look for the upcoming new year are the key objectives of the programme.

All employees shall engage in 5S activities on this day as per the guidelines which will be issued by the Quality Management Section. It is important to take suitable measures to avoid any interruptions to customer services and essential operational activities on SLT Big Cleaning Day.

I highly expect active participation of all employees on this day and wish all the success for SLT Annual Big Cleaning Day Programme.

Janaka R. Abeysinghe
Chief Executive Officer
