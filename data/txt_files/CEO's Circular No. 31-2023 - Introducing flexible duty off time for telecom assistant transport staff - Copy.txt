CEO’s Circular No: 31/ 2023

CEO’s Office, Sri Lanka Telecom PLC, Colombo 01.

2nd May 2023

To All Employees,

INTRODUCING FLEXIBLE DUTY OFF TIME FOR TELECOM ASSISTANT TRANSPORT STAFF

SLT management is pleased to inform that flexible duty off time has been introduced for Telecom Assistant Transport staff who are presently having duty time of 8.30 – 17.30 hrs. Accordingly if an employee reports to work before the assigned IN time of 8.30 hrs, he/she will be allowed to leave the office 15 minutes prior to the assigned OUT time of 17.30 hrs (i.e. at 17.15 hrs), without disturbing daily assigned operational work. This flexible duty off time will come into effect from 01st May 2023.

If the employee requests to perform overtime, the overtime calculation will start from 17:30 hrs onwards. This flexible duty off time is not applicable for employees who have obtained prior approval to perform pre-OT.

For all other administrative purposes, duty time of Telecom Assistant Transport staff will remain as 8.30 – 17.30 hrs.

The heads of respective functional units are required to ensure that work is not disturbed, and customer services are maintained after the implementation of the above.

Janaka R Abeysinghe Chief Executive Officer
