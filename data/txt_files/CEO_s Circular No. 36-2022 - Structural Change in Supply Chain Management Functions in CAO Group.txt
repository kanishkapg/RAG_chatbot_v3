STRUCTURAL CHANGE IN SUPPLY CHAIN MANAGEMENT FUNCTIONS IN CAO GROUP

Please be informed that in line with the revision of delegation of financial authority, the following structural change will be made under the CAO Group with immediate effect.

| Name    | Current Designation | New Structure    |
|---|---|---|
|    |    | New Designation    | Reporting to    |
| Mr. N Navaneethan   | DGM-Material Management & Information Systems | DGM- Commercial Evaluation, Material Management & Information Systems | GM-SCM    |

Accordingly, Mr. Navaneethan will report functionally to DCSCMO / GM-CE for commercial evaluation functions.

New organization structure of the CAO Group - Supply Chain Management Function up to DGM Level is attached as Annexure 01.

Janaka R Abeysinghe Chief Executive Officer
