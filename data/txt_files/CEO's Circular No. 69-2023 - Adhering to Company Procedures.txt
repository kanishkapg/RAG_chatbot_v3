CEO’s Office

26th September 2023

**To: All Staff,**

**Adhering to Company Procedures**

The Code of Conduct & Disciplinary Procedure and Digital Media Policy of Sri Lanka Telecom PLC (SLT)
set out the compliance, guidelines, and best practices to be followed by employees of the Company.

As per the said guidelines the employees should refrain from committing any act that would bring the
Company into disrepute or engage in any activity that would be detrimental to the interests of the Company,
its stakeholders and should abstain from publishing remarks or statements deceptive, misleading, factually
incorrect, or unsubstantiated claims about the Company to print, electronic, or digital media or in public.

Violation of these guidelines will be subjected to appropriate disciplinary action by the disciplinary
authority of Sri Lanka Telecom PLC.

It is expected that all the employees will understand and refrain from engaging in such activities which are
detrimental to the interests of the Company.

**Janaka R Abeysinghe**
**Chief Executive Officer**
