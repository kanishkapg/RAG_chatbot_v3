CEO's Circular No: 84 / 2024

CEO's Office, Sri Lanka Telecom PLC, Colombo 01.

13 September 2024

To All Executive Staff,

INTERNAL PROMOTION FOR THE POST OF MANAGER / BACK-END PROPERTY

MANAGEMENT - A5

Applications are invited from the permanent, confirmed SLT employees who satisfy the undermentioned requirements to be considered for the above post.

A. ELIGIBILITY
Any A6 Grade officer who have completed 03 years in A6 Grade as at 1st September 2024

B. SELECTION METHOD
1. Selection will be made through an examination and an interview.
2. Examination will be held in English Medium.
3. Interview will be held only for successful candidates of the examination for the final selection.

The subjects specified for the examination are as follows:
i) SLT Procedures, Practices & Business Affairs (40 minutes)
ii) Subject Knowledge
    • Part I - Multiple Choice Questions (45 minutes)
    • Part II - Essay Type Questions (1 ¼ hours)

C. PLACEMENT
Selected candidate will be promoted as Manager / Back -End Property Management - A5 and placed at Property Management Section.

D. METHOD OF APPLYING
Eligible candidates should fill in the attached application form and forward to DGM-HR Operations, 7th Floor, HQ Building, Sri Lanka Telecom PLC, Lotus Road, Colombo 01 through the relevant General Manager / Deputy General Manager.

E. CLOSING DATE
Applications should be sent within seven (07) working days of issuing this circular. Applications sent after the closing date will not be considered.
