REVISION OF INTEREST RATE APPLICABLE FOR DISTRESS LOAN

The method for calculating the interest rate applicable for Distress Loan shall be revised from 1st January 2024 and the new method applicable will be 75% of the weekly Average Weighted Prime Lending Rate (AWPLR) declared by the Central Bank at the beginning of the month or 10%, whichever is lower, to be revised monthly.

Accordingly, the new interest rate applicable for the Distress Loan from 1st January 2024 will be 9.1%.

Janaka R. Abeysinghe
Chief Executive Officer
