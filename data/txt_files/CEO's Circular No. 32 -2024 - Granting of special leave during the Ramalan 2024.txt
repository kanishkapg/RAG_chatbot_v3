CEO's Office, Sri Lanka Telecom PLC Colombo 01.
07.03.2024

To All Executives,

Granting of Special Leave during the Ramalan (Ramazan) Season 2024

As the forthcoming Ramalan (Ramazan) Season is due to commence on 12th March 2024 and end on 11th April 2024, kindly make arrangements to enable Muslim employees to take part in prayers and religious activities during this period without disturbing the normal duties.

This special leave can be granted for one and a half hours period for a day either in the morning or afternoon as they wish.

Chief Executive Officer
