CEO's Circular No. 40 / 2022

CEO's Office, Sri Lanka Telecom PLC, Colombo 01.

13th May 2022

To: All SLT Staff,

REVISION OF TRANSPORT, MEAL AND SUBSISTENCE (BATTA) ALLOWANCES: NON-EXECUTIVE STAFF

Prevailing socio-economic conditions in the country has created enormous challenges on company revenue, collections and operating expenditure. In spite of the financial pressure on the company profit and cashflows in the recent past, the management has continued to provide benefits enjoyed by the SLT staff to maintain their financial stability as indicated below.

a. Upward Salary Revision effective from 1st September 2021 as a positive step towards reaching an interim solution to the salary anomalies.
   - 6% Salary increase was previously granted to A7 to A6 grade promotions, and it was increased to 10%
   - 2% Salary increase was previously granted to all non-executive grade promotions, and it was increased to 6%
   - The experience allowance provided to staff recruited from HCS was increased by 25% and added to the basic salary.

b. Payment of 4.5 months basic salary of Bonus for the year 2021 (Bonus of 3.5 fixed number of months basic salary and up to one (01) month performance-based bonus of basic salary subject to a minimum of LKR 150,000/-)

c. Payment of LKR 40,000/- as an ex-gratia payment in December 2021 to all staff as a recognition of the contribution of all employees who performed without interruptions during the COVID 19 pandemic.

d. 10% salary increase (i.e. 8.5% fixed + 1.5% performance based) for the year 2022 effective from 1st January 2022.

e. Payment for unavailed leave for the year 2021 up to a maximum of one (01) month basic salary subject to unavailed leave balances of employees during the year 2021. This payment was made in February 2022.

f. Payment of one (01) month's basic salary in April 2022 subject to a minimum of LKR 70,000/- as an advance bonus payment for all SLT staff.

Despite the above-mentioned increased benefits to staff and even under the current difficult circumstances the company is deeply concerned about the difficulties of the staff. Also, at several discussions with the Trade Unions they brought these difficulties to the attention of the management. Accordingly, the Board of Directors have approved a revision to the Transport, Meal and Subsistence (BATTA) allowances with effect from 1st May 2022 as an immediate relief to all SLT staff while considering the maximum affordable disbursement from the company cash flows.

The dedication and commitment of our employees pave the way to drive our company forward by delivering strong growth & financial performance despite the challenges faced during the past and the management greatly appreciate all staff for their support and cooperation during this journey.

In a context where the SLT's operations are defined as an essential service, the management has already made all measures to continue the business and operations of the company as usual manner without any disruption. Further the Regional, Divisional and the Sectional Management are empowered to take business and operational decisions to ensure business continuity of the company. Hence the employees are requested to join hands with the management to move forward Sri Lanka Telecom PLC to greater heights in these challenging times.
