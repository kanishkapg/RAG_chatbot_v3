RE-ESTABLISHMENT OF ENERGY MANAGEMENT FORUM (EMF) AND

ENHANCING ENERGY MANAGEMENT ACTIVITIES IN SLT

SLT is prioritizing efficient energy management to enhance profitability and minimize environmental impact. Systematic energy management will lower operational costs and support environmental conservation, aligning with SLT's commitment to responsible corporate citizenship. We encourage all employees to actively engage in conserving and efficiently using energy, benefiting both the company and the nation. SLT aims to achieve the following objectives through our energy management initiatives and activities.

1. Increase the use of renewable energy to 35% by 2030.
2. Reduce energy consumption annually in existing facilities.
3. Implementing new facilities according to the Energy Efficiency Building Code of Sri Lanka.
4. Reach carbon footprint reduction rate 25,000 metric tons per year by 2030.

Accordingly, SLT management has reviewed.

1). The Energy Management Forum, co-chaired by the CAO and CNO and consisting of a cross-functional teams, is tasked with analyzing our energy use, identifying initiatives and activities to optimize energy usage.

2). Energy Ambassadors are appointed to drive, implement, sustain energy management activities, monitor, and report to EMF.
