CEO's Office, Sri Lanka Telecom PLC, Colombo 01.

28th April 2022

To All CXO, DCXO & GMM,

POST OF CHIEF PEOPLE OFFICER

Applications are invited from officers who satisfy the undermentioned requirements to be considered for the above post.

1. CXOs, DCXOs and GMM in A1 Grade

CXOs, DCXOs, GMM in A1 Grade and in Engineering, Accounting, Legal, HR and Marketing professions who wish to be considered for the above position may apply.

2. DCXOs and GMM in A2 Grade

(a) Engineering Profession
Corporate Membership of IESL/EE(UK)/I MechE(UK)/Institution of Civil Engineers (UK) with 5 years' experience in A2 Grade in SLT.

(b) Accounting Profession
Associate Membership of ICA/CIMA/ACCA with 5 years' experience in A2 Grade in SLT.

(c) Legal Profession
An Attorney-at-law and Notary Public with 5 years' experience in A2 Grade in SLT.

(d) HR Profession
Degree of a recognized university with Full Membership of CIPM Sri Lanka or equivalent with 5 years' experience in A2 Grade in SLT.

(e) Marketing Profession
MCIM (UK) with Chartered Marketer Status with 05 years' experience in A2 Grade in SLT.

An MBA in Business Management / HRM from a recognized university with experience in people management will be an added qualification.

3. Selection Method
Selection will be by an interview.

4. Placement
After fulfilling the selection criteria of Executive Staff Recruitment and Promotion Scheme (SRPS) and successful completion of trial period, if the selected officer is in A2 Grade he/she will be placed in A1 Grade. Further, if the selected officer is in A1 Grade, his/her grade will remain unchanged.

5. Method of Applying
Eligible candidates should fill the attached application form and forward to Chief Executive Officer through the relevant Supervising Officer.

6. Closing Date
Applications should be sent within 07 working days of issuing this Circular.

Janaka R Abeysinghe
Chief Executive Officer
