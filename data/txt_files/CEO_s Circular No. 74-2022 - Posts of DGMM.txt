CEO's Circular No: 74/2022

To: All Executive Staff,

POSTS OF DEPUTY GENERAL MANAGER

1. DGM - WIRELINE ACCESS NW OPERATIONS
2. DGM- CLOUD SERVICES
3. DGM - WIRELESS NW PLANNING
4. DGM- CARRIER BUSINESS (DOMESTIC)
5. DGM-GOVERNMENT BUSINESS
6. DGM-ENTERPRISE BUSINESS (LARGE)

Applications are invited from permanent executive staff of SLT, who satisfy the under mentioned requirements to be considered for the above posts.

1. ELIGIBILITY FOR TRANSFER

DGMs (A2, A3, A4) in engineering profession who wish to be transferred to above positions shall apply.

2. ELIGIBILITY FOR PROMOTION

2.1 FOR OFFICERS PRESENTLY IN A3 GRADE:

A3 grade officers in engineering profession who wish to be considered for the above positions shall apply.

2.2 FOR OFFICERS PRESENTLY IN A4 GRADE:

(a) Engineering Profession

(i) Corporate Member of IESL/IET (IEE) UK/IMechE (UK)/Institution of Civil Engineers (UK) WITH 03 years' experience in A4 Grade in SLT OR

(ii) An Engineering Degree / Associate Membership of IESL/IET(IEE) UK /IMechE (UK)/ Institution of Civil Engineers (UK) WITH 05 years' experience in A4 Grade in SLT OR

(iii) 7 years' engineering experience in A4 Grade in SLT.

2.3 FOR OFFICERS PRESENTLY IN AS GRADE:

(a) Engineering Profession

(i) Corporate Membership of IESL/IET(IEE)UK/IMechE (UK)/Institution of Civil Engineers (UK) WITH 03 years' experience in AS Grade in SLT OR
(ii) An Engineering Degree/ Associate Membership of IESL/IET(IEE)UK/IMechE (UK)/ Institution of Civil Engineers (UK) WITH 06 years' experience in AS Grade in SLT OR
(iii) 08 years' experience in AS Grade in SLT

2.4 Officers in A4 Grade who do not possess required number of experience in A4 Grade under category 2.2 will be eligible under category 2.3 by adding the number of years' experience in AS Grade and A4 Grade together and those officers may also apply.

3 SELECTION METHOD

Selection will be by an interview.

4 PLACEMENT

(a) Transfer (As per Section 1)
Selected Deputy General Managers will be transferred for the above mentioned positions.

(b) Promotion (As per Section 2)
Selected candidates will be initially appointed in Acting capacity. Subsequent to serving in the acting capacity for 06 months' period, if the selected officer is in A4 Grade, he /she will be placed in A3 Grade and if the selected officer is in A5 Grade he / she will be placed in A4 Grade subject to provisions available in SRPS. Further, if the selected officer in A3 Grade, his / her Grade will remain unchanged.

5 METHOD OF APPLYING

Eligible candidates should fill in the attached application form and forward to GM/HR & IR through the relevant Supervising Officer.

6 CLOSING DATE

Applications should be sent within 07 working days of issuing this Circular.

Janaka R Abeysinghe
Chief Executive Officer
