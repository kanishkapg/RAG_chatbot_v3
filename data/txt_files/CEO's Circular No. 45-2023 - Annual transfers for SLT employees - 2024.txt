To All Staff,

Annual Transfers for SLT Employees – 2024

Applications for annual transfers for the year 2024 are invited from Non - Executive Staff (A16 – A7) and Security Staff (S5 – S2) who are eligible under the SLT Transfer Procedure.

Staff in combined categories (Customer Care Officers, Customer Relations Officers and IT Coordinators who perform Customer Care job role) will be considered together for Annual Transfer Scheme 2024.

Annual Transfer Application process is automated via ERP and all staff shall use ERP based Transfer application form to submit Annual Transfer requests for the year 2024.

Relevant guideline is attached as Annex 01.

Annual Transfer Application Closing date will be 21st July 2023 and applications received after the closing date will not be entertained.

Special note:
If an employee completed the maximum stipulated period in any station as at 31st December 2023 such employee shall be considered for transfer in order to give an opportunity for another employee. However, if he/she has any problem or difficulties regarding the transfer, such difficulties shall be mentioned in the transfer application itself with supporting documents, for the consideration by the respective transfer board.
