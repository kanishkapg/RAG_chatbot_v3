CEO's Circular No: 42/2022

CEO's Office, Sri Lanka Telecom.PLC, Colombo 01.

3 Oth May 2022

To All SLT Staff,

Amendment to the CEO's Circular 30/2022 on Internal Recruitment.

Qualification requirement of 'G C E (A/L) Examination with 03 passes in one sitting' for the internal recruitment of IT & Network Officer/Software Developer-A9 and Marketing Officer-A9 stated in the CEO's Circular No 30/2022 dated 7th April 2022 will be excluded. This amendment is applicable only for the above circular.

Accordingly new applicants can submit applications through ERP system (as stated in the original circular) within 07 working days of issuing this circular. Scan copies of relevant certificates should be attached.

Janaka R Abeysinghe Chief Executive Officer
