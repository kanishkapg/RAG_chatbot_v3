INTRODUCTION OF MEMBERSHIP REIMBURSEMENT PROCEDURE

Membership Reimbursement Procedure (Document No: HRSTP/MRP/023) sets out to introduce a system to recognize, encourage and motivate SLT staff to be professionally qualified by acquiring professional and academic qualifications in multi-disciplinary fields relevant to SLT's business. This will facilitate for developing a world-class workforce within the organization required to support for the achievement of organizational objectives. This Procedure shall be applicable only for executive staff.

The Membership Reimbursement Procedure of SLT PLC (Document No: HRSTP/MRP/023) is available at SLT intranet (General>SLT Common Document Portal>Policies>Human Resources (CPO)> Membership Reimbursement Procedure).

Above Procedure will supersedes all previous circulars, procedures, approvals and memos hereto issued in this regard.

Janaka R. Abeysinghe  
Chief Executive Officer
