To All SLT Staff,

Application for the Part Time NVQ Level 4 Telecommunication Technician Program in SLT (open only for SLT Employees' Children).

Applications are invited from children of SLT employees who are possessing below stated qualifications and fulfill the other eligibility criteria as at 28th February 2022 to be considered for program stated above.

1. Qualification & Eligibility Criteria.

a) 06 passes at the G.C.E. (O/L) exam including Sinhala/ Tamil and Mathematics and 03 credit passes in not more than two sitting.
b) Upper Age Limit is 35 Years.
c) Male Applicant.

2. Criteria and other conditions applicable to employees to make eligible their children to the scheme.

a) SLT Permanent employees who are currently working in SLT and having more than 10 years of confirmed and satisfactory service in SLT as at 28th February 2022.
b) SLT Permanent retired employees who were working in SLT during last 10 years as at 28th February 2022 and having completed more than 10 years of confirmed and satisfactory service in SLT at the date of retirement.
c) The benefit will only be given to one child of SLT employee. Employees whose child/children were recruited under the previous "employees' children scheme" are not eligible to the scheme.
d) When both husband & wife (parents of applicant) are employees/retired employee(s) in SLT, only one child is eligible to the scheme.

3. Method of Selection.

Selection will be made through an Aptitude Test and Interview.

4. Conditions relevant to the NVQ Level 4 Telecommunication Technician Program.

4.1 Type of the program: Part Time.
4.2 Date: Every Saturday (09.00 to 17:00)
4.3 Duration: 3 years.
4.4 Medium : English
4.5 In-plant Training: 3 years (every Monday to Friday). Training Period will not be extended after three years on any reason.
4.6 Internship Allowance: LKR 1000/= per day will be paid during the period of in-plant training.
4.7 SLT TDC will conduct six months special English Course for the selected applicants.
4.8 On successful completion of the Theory and implant training, participants will be eligible for NVQ4 final assessment to be conducted by Tertiary and Vocational Education Commission (TVEC).

a) Those who successfully complete the program and obtain NVQ 4 certificate issued by TVEC and possess a valid driving license to drive minimum "B" vehicle class motor vehicles will be considered for recruitment as a Technician-A15 to the SLT based on the availability of vacancies.

5. Course Fee.

Total course fee for the program is LKR 100,800.00. This will be deducted in 36 equal installments (LKR 2,800/- per month) from the training allowance to be paid as internship allowance to selected applicants.

6. Method of Applying

Application should be sent via online. Visit www.sltmobitel.lk (careers page) and complete the online application on your own, submit with curriculum vitae together with scanned copies of specimen application form attached herewith as annexure 1, birth, educational and other relevant certificates within 7 working days of issuing this circular.

Applications without supporting documents will not be entertained and applications sent by post will not be accepted.

Application link is available at the SLT website: www.sltmobitel.lk (visit careers page). Attachments file size should not exceed 15 MB.

Sri Lanka Telecom PLC is an equal opportunity employer and any type of canvassing will be a disqualification. Only shortlisted applicants will be contacted by HR.

Janaka R Abeysinghe
Chief Executive Officer
