CEO’s Office, Sri Lanka Telecom PLC, Colombo 01.

To All Employees,

PAYMENT FOR UNAVAILED LEAVE OF SLT EMPLOYEES - 2023

Please follow the under mentioned procedure in making payment for unavailed leave for permanent SLT employees for the year 2023.

1. One month's salary should be paid for any kind of unavailed leave of maximum 21 days. If unavailed leave during the year 2023 is less than 21 days, an amount equivalent to 1/21 of one month's basic salary should be deducted for each such day.
   Eg: If an employee has 07 days of unavailed leave, incentive payment should be calculated as follows;
    Basic Salary X 7
    21

2. The unavailed leave payment will be paid in accordance with the policy for Annual salary revision, Bonus & Leave encashment in SLT based on the December 2023 basic salary.

3. Computation of sick leave entitlement for those who have been made permanent in their appointment in the year 2023 and Computation of annual leave entitlement for year 2023 of those who have been made permanent during 2022 should be as follows.

(a) Sick Leave / Annual Leave:
   i. 14 days for those who have been made permanent between 01st January and 31st March
   ii. 10 days for those who have been made permanent between 01st April and 30th June
   iii. 07 days for those who have been made permanent between 01st July and 30th September
   iv. 04 days for those who have been made permanent between 01st October and 31st December

(b) Casual Leave:
   For those who were appointed in the year 2023, one day leave should be counted for a unit of two working months' period.

4. Please note that it will not be possible to pay the "Unavailed leave Allowance" to employees attached to offices that have not furnished their computerized leave data up to 31st December 2023 to the Leave Section.

5. Once the employees receive payment for unavailed leave, the entitlement for this leave will not exist in the future.

6. All GM / DGM / RTOM and other relevant officers are requested to provide the necessary leave details on time.
