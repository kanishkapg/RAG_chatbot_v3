CEO’s Circular No: 94/2023

CEO’s Office, Sri Lanka Telecom PLC, Colombo 01
14th December 2023

To All Staff,

MAINTAINING UNINTERRUPTED OPERATIONS & CUSTOMER SERVICES

We wish to reemphasize several instructions issued to all staff via CEO’s Circular No: 86/2023 dated 23rd November 2023.

SLT’s operation is essential for the continuation of critical services and business activities of our valued customers. Therefore, it is the responsibility of all employees to discharge their assigned business/operational duties with no disturbance to SLTMobitel operations and customer services. We all are aware that any interruption in our operations will adversely affect the image of the company’s business.

The management has already made all the measures to continue the business and operations of the company in the usual manner. Further, all the Chief Officers, the Regional, Divisional and the Sectional management are empowered to take the business and operational decisions to ensure business continuity of the company.

The management seeks cooperation of all staff to refrain from engaging in any activity which is detrimental to the interests of the company and to report to their workstations and engage in the assigned duties as directed by their immediate supervisors to maintain uninterrupted operations and customer services.

Employees are hereby strongly advised not to violate any company rules and procedures, and management will be compelled to take disciplinary actions against employees for any such violations.

Janaka R Abeysinghe
Chief Executive Officer
