Amendment on Overseas Leave Procedure - Overseas No Pay Leave for Employment

Overseas no pay leave for the confirmed permanent SLT employees will be granted for the purpose of enhancement of their professional and educational level. Accordingly Employee with minimum 05 years confirmed permanent service of SLT and maximum age limit of 55 years are eligible for applying Overseas No Pay Leave for Employment.

It was proposed to reconsider/revisit the minimum eligible period required for foreign employment by considering the prevailing economic situation in the country and by considering the Government initiatives.

Hence, employees with minimum of 03 years confirmed permanent service in SLT and the maximum age limit of 55 years will be eligible for apply overseas No pay Leave for the Employment subject to other conditions stipulated in the procedure. Further no replacements will be provided in lieu of the above and the overseas leave will be granted on work rearrangement basis only.

Relevant supervisor should ensure that internal arrangements are made to overlook the duties of the officer who goes on overseas no pay leave.

This arrangement to be effective until 31st December 2022.

Janaka R Abeysinghe
Chief Executive Officer
