CEO's Circular No: 37/2024

CEO's Office, Sri Lanka Telecom PLC, Colombo 01.
10 April 2024

To All Staff,

APPOINTMENT OF CHIEF LEGAL OFFICER OF SLTMOBITEL GROUP

Please be informed that Mr. Namal Rathnayake, Deputy Chief Legal Officer & SGM – Regulatory Affairs of SLTMobitel Group has been appointed as Chief Legal Officer (CLO) of SLTMobitel Group with immediate effect. Mr. Ratnayake will report to CEO through COO-SLT and COO-Mobitel for the respective SLTMobitel Group functions.

Mr. Namal Ratnayake joined Mobitel as Senior Manager-Legal in 2005 and has served in the capacity of SGM-Legal, and Deputy Chief Legal Officer of SLTMobitel Group, before being appointed as the Chief Legal Officer of SLTMobitel Group. Prior to joining Mobitel Mr. Ratnayake served at Hayleys Electronics Ltd., from 1996 to 1998 and at the Attorney General's Department from 1998 to 2005. He has 27 years of experience in the legal field.

He holds a LL.B degree from the University of Colombo, Sri Lanka, and is an Attorney-at-Law.

Mr. Rathnayake will work in parallel with Mrs. H. D. A. S. A. Wijekoon until her retirement on the 14th April 2024 to facilitate smooth transition of duties.

The position of Deputy Chief Legal Officer will be abolished with immediate effect.

GM-Regulatory Affairs of SLT, DGM-Legal of SLT, Senior Manager–Legal of Mobitel, and Manager – Regulatory Affairs of Mobitel will directly report to the CLO.

We wish him all the success in his new job role.

Janaka R. Abeysinghe
Chief Executive Officer
