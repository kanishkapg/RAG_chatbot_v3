CEO’s Circular No: 02/2023

CEO’s Office, Sri Lanka Telecom PLC, Colombo 01, 4th January 2023

To: All Staff

RESUMPTION OF OPERATIONS INTO NORMALCY

I sincerely thank you for your support, dedication, and commitment to continue SLT’s business operations, during the difficult times in 2022.

As the country has become normal, SLT PLC shall resume its activities in full capacity and revert back to normalcy.

Hence, all employees are requested to report to duties as per the normal duty times / standard duty rosters to their respective workstations with effect from 09th January 2023.

However, a grace period will be granted to continue the prevailing working arrangement until 1st February 2023, to employees who have practical difficulties in reporting to their regular workstations subject to approval by the respective DGMM and above supervising officers.

Janaka R Abeysinghe Chief Executive Officer
