CEO’s Office, Sri Lanka Telecom PLC, Colombo 01.

To All Staff,

ORGANIZATIONAL CHANGES IN REGIONAL OPERATIONS STRUCTURE

Mr. A. Kirupakaran, Chief Officer, Regional Operations (CO-RO) retires as of 25th July 2024. Consequent to Mr. A. Kirupakaran's retirement, CO-RO position is abolished and the following organizational changes will take place with immediate effect.

Mr. R. M. A. C. B Rathnayaka, Deputy Chief Officer – Regional Operations (DCO – RO) will report directly to Mr. M. S. Samarasinha, Chief Network Officer.

General Managers – Regional Operations (GM-ROs) of Region 1, Region 2, Region 3, Metro Regions and General Manager / Operations Support will continue to report to DCO – RO.

DCO- RO will overlook the duties of the position of GM – RO (Region 1), until a suitable officer is appointed.

Updated organizational structure of Network Group is shown in Annexure 1.

Janaka R Abeysinghe
Chief Executive Officer
