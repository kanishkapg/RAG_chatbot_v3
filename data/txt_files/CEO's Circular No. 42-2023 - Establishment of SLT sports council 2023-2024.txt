To All SLT Employees,

ESTABLISHMENT OF SLT SPORTS COUNCIL 2023/2024

The management of Sri Lanka Telecom PLC (SLT) has decided to establish a Sports Council to act as the governing body and advisory committee responsible for overseeing and promoting SLT sports-related activities within the organization.

Given are the functions of the Sports Council:

- Promote employee participation in sports in the organization.
- Organize “SLT Games”.
- Encourage the current Sports Associations in SLT to organize events and participate for national and international competitions.
- Take initiatives to encourage employees of all ages and abilities to engage in sports activities.
- Distribute funds among Sports Associations to facilitate equipment acquisition, facility arrangement and to take part in competitions, considering budget availability.
- Preparing employees for national and international competitions
