CEO's Circular No: 54/2022

CEO's Office, Sri Lanka Telecom PLC, Colombo 01  
7th July 2022  

To All Staff,

APPOINTMENT OF CHIEF OPERATING OFFICER

Please be informed that Mr. H. K. S. K. Abeysekara is appointed as the Chief Operating Officer (COO) of Sri Lanka Telecom PLC with immediate effect.

Mr. Abeysekara will be reporting to Chief Executive Officer of SLT PLC.

Further, Mr. Abeysekara will overlook the duties of Chief Administrative Officer (CAO) until a suitable replacement is made.

Mr. Abeysekara joined SLT in 1998 and has served for SLT in the capacity of Regional Telecommunication Manager, Provincial Deputy General Manager, Regional General Manager, Deputy Chief Regional Operating Officer and Chief Asset, Property & Security Management Officer before being appointed as the Chief Administrative Officer and the Chief Operating Officer - Designate of SLT PLC.

He holds a BSc (Eng.) in Electrical and Electronics Engineering from University of Peradeniya, Sri Lanka and is a Chartered Engineer and a Fellow of the Institution of Engineers Sri Lanka.

We wish him all success in his new job role.

Janaka R Abeysinghe  
Chief Executive Officer
