CEO’s Circular No: 64/2023

CEO’s Office, Sri Lanka Telecom PLC, Colombo 01
04th September 2023

To All Staff,

ORGANIZATIONAL RE-ALIGNMENT

Please note that the following organizational structural re-alignment will be implemented with immediate effect in view of strengthening the organizational digital transformation strategy and execution.

| Name    | Present Structure    | New Structure    |
|---|---|---|
|    | Designation    | Reporting to    | Designation    | Reporting to    |
| Mr. A. P. Galagoda   | Chief Officer - Network Strategy & Projects | CNO    | Chief Officer – Digital Transformation | CTO    |
| Mr. J C Harambearachchi | GM-Digital Platforms | CTO    | GM-Digital Platforms | Chief Officer – Digital Transformation | CTO    |
| Mrs. K. D S I Siriwardena | GM – Digital Transformation Projects | CTO    | GM – Digital Transformation Projects | CTO    |

Further, with the above change, GM- Network Strategy & Planning, SGM – Network Planning & Optimization (Mobitel), GM-Projects, GM-National Fiberization Program & FTTH, shall directly report to CNO.

Accordingly, the Chief Officer - Network Strategy & Projects position will be abolished.

Janaka R. Abeysinghe  
Chief Executive Officer
