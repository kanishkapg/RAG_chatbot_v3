CEO's Circular No: 04/2024

CEO's Office, Sri Lanka Telecom PLC, Colombo 01.
10th January 2024

To All Executive Staff,

PAYMENT OF A SALARY ADVANCE FOR ALL EXECUTIVE STAFF AS A HARDSHIP MITIGATION CONCESSION

SLTMobitel Board has approved to disburse a salary advance to all executive staff of SLTMobitel Group, who have applied to obtain a salary advance.

Therefore, a salary advance of up to two (02) months of basic salary, subject to a maximum of LKR 400,000/- will be paid on request to any executive employee of SLTMobitel Group who is eligible and has applied for such an advance. This salary advance will be fully recovered by the company by deducting from employees salary after a 3 month grace period (from April 2024 onwards) in equal 9 monthly instalments.

The deadline for application for the salary advance is January 15, 2024.

Eligible executive employees who wish to apply for this salary advance shall apply for the same via the path given in the ERP system.

Direct application for employees

SLT Employee Self-Service -> Loan -> Cash Advance Request - Executives

Janaka R Abeysinghe
Chief Executive Officer
