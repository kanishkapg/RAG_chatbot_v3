To All Staff,

Energy Conservation to manage Energy Crisis

By considering the prevailing situation in the country and SLT being a responsible corporate citizen, management of SLT requests all the employees to give priority to manage power and conserve energy at office and at your residences to help the company and the country to conquer this difficult period. In view of this, you are requested to adhere to following best practices.

- Switch off all unnecessary lights.
- Switch off comfort ACs as much as possible in offices. Set the room temperatures above 26°C if air conditioners are used.
- Switch off lights after use of washrooms.
- Switch off lights after use of meal rooms.
- Switch off lights in corridors, passages, or lobby areas.
- Close the doors properly, when air conditioner is ON.
- Find out and report any air conditioner leakages, Power wastage to Power & AC Operations team or respective network engineers.

You may contact Mr. Prasanna Weththasinghe, Engineer – Energy Management (Mobile 0714179062, email -prasannaw@slt.com.lk) for any suggestions or improvements.

As responsible employees of SLT, management expects that employees will support SLT and the country to save electricity to manage the present energy crisis.
