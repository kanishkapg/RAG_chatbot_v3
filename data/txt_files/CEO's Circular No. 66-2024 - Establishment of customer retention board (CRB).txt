To All Staff,

Establishment of Customer Retention Board (CRB)

In this highly competitive market where we conduct our business, retaining customers for the long term is of paramount importance. Recently, FTTH customer churn has increased due to both external factors and internal inefficiencies. To address this issue and ensure healthy growth of customer base with a significant market share, Customer Retention Board (CRB) is established with immediate effect. The CRB is fully empowered to make decisions and take actions to retain customers for the long term. It is specifically responsible for addressing churn in the wired fixed-line services of the SLTMobitel Group.
