To All Staff,

Annual Transfers for SLT Employees - 2023

Applications for annual transfers for the year 2023 are invited from Non - Executive Staff (A16 - A7) who are eligible under the SLT Transfer Procedure.

Staff in combined categories (Customer Care Officers, Customer Relations Officers and IT Coordinators who perform Customer Care job role) will be considered together for Annual Transfer Scheme 2023.

Annual Transfer Application process is automated via ERP and all staff in A16 to A7 grades shall use ERP based Transfer application form to submit Annual Transfer requests for the year 2023. Relevant guideline is attached as Annex 01.

Annual Transfer Application Closing date will be 31st August 2022 and applications received after the closing date will not be entertained.

Contact Details: Deputy General Manager/HR Operations, 7th Floor, HQ Building, Sri Lanka Telecom PLC, Lotus Road, Colombo 01. Tel: 011-2334377

For more details:

Manager/Regional HR Partner II 0112021648 Other Officers of HR Operations (Sandamali & Nadeeka) 0112021495 / 0112439431

Please note that transfer applications received as per this Circular will be considered only for the Annual Transfers for the year 2023.

Janaka R. Abeysinghe Chief Executive Officer
