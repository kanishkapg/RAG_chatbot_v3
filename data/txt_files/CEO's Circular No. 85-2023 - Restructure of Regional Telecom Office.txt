Restructure of Regional Telecom Office

Regional Telecom Office (RTO) and Teleshop play the vital role of physical interaction with customers, and building SLT Mobitel brand to the public.

It is important to enhance the services at these customer touch points to satisfy customer requirements aligned with corporate vision and objectives.

Accordingly, Regional Telecom Office Manager (RTOM) will be re-designated as Regional Manager (RM), and the new organizational structure of RTO is as follows:

Regional Manager (RM)

Regional Accountant

Customer Service Manager (CSM)

Sales Manager (SM)

External Channel Manager (ECM)

Customer Retention Manager (CRM)

Teleshop Managers (TSM)

Redefined core job functions of RM and Line Managers reporting to RM are listed in Annexure-1. New job descriptions will be issued to them individually.

Each RTO is classified into category A, B and C, while identified Teleshops are classified as Super Grade Teleshops as listed in Annexure-2 mainly based on customer base, revenue generation and sales potential.

Grades of Line Managers based on RTO category and Teleshop category are as follows;

| RTO Category | Line Manager Grade |
|---|---|
| A    | A5/A6/A7    |
| B    | A6/A7    |
| C    | A6/A7/A8/A9    |

| Teleshop Type | Teleshop Manager Grade |
|---|---|
| Super Grade Teleshop | A5/A6/A7    |
| Other Teleshops | A6/A7    |

Number of Line Managers in a category C RTO is limited to three excluding TSMs.

This circular will be implemented with immediate effect.

Janaka R. Abeysinghe
Chief Executive Officer
