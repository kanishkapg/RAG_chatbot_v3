CHANGES TO THE REPORTING STRUCTURE OF PROVINCIAL ACCOUNTANTS

The following changes to the reporting structure of provincial accountants will be implemented effective from 01st January 2025.

- Administrative Reporting: Provincial Accountants will continue to report administratively to the respective Regional Business General Managers (GMs)
- Functional Reporting: Provincial Accountants will report functionally to the General Managers (GMs) in the Finance Group. This change aims to strengthen financial oversight, ensure adherence to financial policies, and promote consistency in financial operations across regions.

All relevant officers are requested to comply with this change and adjust workflows as necessary in accordance with the details outlined in the new reporting structure (Annexure 1).

Janaka R Abeysinghe
