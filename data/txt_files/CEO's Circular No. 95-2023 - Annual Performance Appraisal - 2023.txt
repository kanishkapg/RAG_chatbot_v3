**ANNUAL PERFORMANCE APPRAISAL-2023**

Please be informed that the annual performance appraisals for the year 2023 will commence with effective from 01.01.2024 for the evaluation period of 01st January 2023 to 31st December 2023.

**Appraisal Process**
Appraisal process consists of 4 steps, namely self-appraisal (appraise), supervisor appraisal (main appraiser), supervisor’s peer appraisal (2 peers) and reviewer appraisal. Approval process should be completed via ERP System through relevant protocol with the approval of respective Chief Officer as per the appraisal time plan specified below. Before finalizing the ratings, respective Chief Officer shall ensure that the distribution of the individual performances within the group should be indicated the overall performance of the group.

**Appraisal Method**
Appraisals of all staff should be carried out through the ERP system. All supervising officers are responsible to ensure completion of performance appraisal in the ERP system for all their subordinates

**Appraisal Interview**
Reviewer has to ensure completion of year-end performance appraisal interview before his/her reviewer evaluation and conduct a focused discussion in an undisturbed environment. It is compulsory to communicate the relevant appraisal rating to the employees with feedback.

**Performance Based Training**
Training and development plays a key role to enhance the ability of the employees to perform. Supervising officers may record and recommend training and/or development activities for their subordinates via ERP after completion of the appraisal.

**Appraisal Time Plan**

| Appraisal stage    | Time plan    |
|---|---|
| Self    | from 1st January 2024 to 05th January 2024 |
| Immediate Supervisor/Main Appraiser   | on or before 12th January 2024 |
| Supervisor Peer    | on or before 19th January 2024 |
| Reviewer    | on or before 25th January 2024 |
| Approval of relevant Manager/DGM/GM/CXO | on or before 31st January 2024 |
