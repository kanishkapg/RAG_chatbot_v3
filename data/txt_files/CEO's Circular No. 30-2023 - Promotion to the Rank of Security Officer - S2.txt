CEO’s Circular No:  3O/2023    
CEO’s Office, Sri Lanka Telecom PLC, Colombo 01.
24th April 2023

To All Security Staff,

**PROMOTION TO THE RANK OF SECURITY OFFICER – S2**

Applications are invited from the permanent, confirmed SLT Security Staff who satisfy the under mentioned requirements to be considered for the above mentioned position.

**A. ELIGIBILITY**

- 10 years confirmed & satisfactory service in SLT with minimum 01 year service in S3 salary grade as a Patrol Leader as at 31st March 2023
  AND
- Should have followed at least 03 prescribed training courses conducted by SLT Security Division whilst serving as a Patrol Leader

**B. SELECTION METHOD**

Selection will be made through an interview.

**C. PLACEMENT**

Selected candidate will be promoted as Security Officer S2 and placed as Security Officer - SLT Head Office

**D. METHOD OF APPLYING**

Eligible candidates should fill in the attached application form and forward to DGM/HR Operations 7th Floor, HQ Building, Sri Lanka Telecom PLC, Lotus Road, Colombo 01 through the relevant General Manager / Deputy General Manager.

**E. CLOSING DATE**

Applications should be sent within seven (07) working days of issuing this circular. Applications sent after the closing date will not be considered.

---

**Janaka R Abeysinghe Chief Executive Officer**
