To All SLT Employees,

ANNUAL PERFORMANCE APPRAISAL-2024

Please be informed that the annual performance appraisals for the year 2024 will commence with effective from 01.01.2025 and the effective duration for the evaluation is from 01st January 2024 to 31st December 2024.

Appraisal Process

Appraisal process consists of 4 steps, namely self-appraisal (Appraisee), supervisor appraisal (main appraiser), supervisor's peers appraisal and reviewer appraisal. Appraisal process should be completed via ERP system through relevant protocol with the approval of respective Chief Officer on or before the date specified under the time plan below. Before finalizing the ratings, respective Chief Officer shall ensure the distribution of the individual performances within the group should be indicated the overall performance of the group.

Appraisal Method

Appraisals of all staff should be carried out through the ERP system. All supervising officers are responsible to ensure completion of performance appraisal in the ERP system for all their subordinates.

Management has decided to retain the Quantitative Rating Scale used for the 2023 evaluation. As a result, the Quantitative Rating Scale outlined in CEO's Circular 62/2024, dated 28th June 2024, will not be implemented. Table 1 below shows the rating scale to be used for the 2024 calculations.

| Achievement (X) out of 100 % | Reference Rating | Definition |
|---|---|---|
| X > 100%    | 5    | Outstanding    |
| 85% ≤ X ≤ 100%    | 4    | Superior    |
| 70% ≤ X < 85%    | 3    | Satisfactory    |
| 50% ≤ X < 70%    | 2    | Need Improvements    |
| X < 50%    | 1    | Unsatisfactory    |

Table 1: Quantitative Rating Scale for the year 2024 evaluation.

Appraisal Interview

Reviewer has to ensure completion of year-end performance appraisal interview before his/her reviewer evaluation and conduct a focused discussion in an undisturbed environment. It is compulsory to communicate the relevant appraisal rating to the employees with feedback.

Performance Based Training

Training and development a key role to enhance the ability of the employees to perform. Supervising officers may record and recommend training and/or development activities for their subordinates via ERP after completion of the appraisal.
