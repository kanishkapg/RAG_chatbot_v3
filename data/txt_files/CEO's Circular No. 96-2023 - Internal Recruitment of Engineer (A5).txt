CEO’s Circular No: 96/2023

CEO’s Office, Sri Lanka Telecom PLC, Colombo 01.
18th December 2023

To All SLT Employees,

Internal Recruitment of Engineer (A5)

Applications are invited from permanent & confirmed SLT employees who possess the following qualifications for the post stated above.

1. Eligibility Criteria & Qualifications:

a) Four-year Degree in BSc Engineering/ Bachelor of Technology (Telecommunication/Electrical & Electronic/ Computer Engineering/ Information Engineering) from a University/Institute recognized by UGC and Institute of Engineers, Sri Lanka, equivalent to SLQF 6.
   OR
b) Associate Member of the Institution of Engineers, Sri Lanka.
   AND
c) Three years of permanent confirmed and satisfactory service in SLT as at 30th November 2023.

2. Method of Selection:

Selection will be made through a written examination (English Medium) and interview.

3. Method of Applying:

Application should be submitted through ERP System to reach HR Strategies & Policy Section within 07 days of issuing this circular. Scan copies of relevant certificates should be attached.

ERP Application Path: ERP >SLT Employee Self-Service>Other Requests>Internal Recruitments>Click on ‘Create Application’>Select the Post Applied>Specify the ‘Vacancy Position/s Applied> Attach Relevant Certificates>Add Qualifications>Click on ‘Create’>Click on ‘Application Number’>tick the ‘Declaration’>Submit.
(Application will be sent to the immediate executive supervisor for recommendation and to the HR thereafter).

Applications sent without supporting documents will not be entertained. Correspondence will only be with the shortlisted applicants.

Janaka R. Abeysinghe
Chief Executive Officer
