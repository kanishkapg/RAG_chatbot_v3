POST OF DEPUTY GENERAL MANAGER – EMPLOYEE RELATIONS

Applications are invited from permanent executive staff of SLT, who satisfy the under mentioned requirements to be considered for the above post.

1. ELIGIBILITY FOR TRANSFER

DGMs (A2, A3, A4) in accounting / engineering / marketing / legal & HR professions who wish to be transferred to above position shall apply.

2. ELIGIBILITY FOR PROMOTION

2.1 FOR OFFICERS PRESENTLY IN A3 GRADE:

A3 grade officers in accounting /engineering / marketing / legal & HR professions who wish to be considered for the above position shall apply.

2.2 FOR OFFICERS PRESENTLY IN A4 GRADE:

(a) Accounting Profession

(i) Associate Membership of ICA/CIMA/ACCA WITH 03 years' Accounting / Auditing experience in A4 Grade in SLT OR

(ii) Intermediate qualification of ICA/CIMA/ACCA WITH 05 years' Accounting / Auditing experience in A4 Grade in SLT OR

(iii) 7 years' Accounting / Auditing experience in A4 Grade in SLT

(b) Engineering Profession

(i) Corporate Member of IESL/IET (IEE) UK/IMechE (UK)/Institution of Civil Engineers (UK) WITH 03 years' experience in A4 Grade in SLT OR

(ii) An Engineering Degree / Associate Membership of IESL/IET(IEE) UK /IMechE (UK)/ Institution of Civil Engineers (UK) WITH 05 years' experience in A4 Grade in SLT OR

(iii) 7 years' engineering experience in A4 Grade in SLT.

CEO's Circular No: 025 /2022

(c) Legal Profession

(i) An Attorney-at-Law and Notary Public WITH 05 years' experience in A4 Grade in SLT

(d) Human Resources Profession
(i) Degree of a recognized university with Full Membership of CIPM Sri Lanka or equivalent WITH 03 years' experience in A4 Grade in SLT OR
(ii) A Degree of a recognized university or Associate Membership of CIPM Sri Lanka or equivalent WITH 05 years' experience in A4 Grade in SLT OR
(iii) 7 years' experience in A4 Grade in SLT

(e) Marketing Profession:
(i) MCIM(UK) with Chartered Marketer Status WITH 03 years' experience in A4 Grade in SLT OR
(ii) MCIM(UK) WITH 05 years' experience in A4 Grade in SLT OR
(iii) 07 years' experience in A4 Grade in SLT

2.3 FOR OFFICERS PRESENTLY IN A5 GRADE:

(a) Accounting Profession

(i) Associate Membership of ICA/CIMA/ACCA WITH 03 years' Accounting / Auditing experience in A5 Grade in SLT OR
(ii) Intermediate qualification of ICA/CIMA/ACCA WITH 06 years' Accounting / Auditing experience in A5 Grade in SLT OR
(iii) 08 years' Accounting / Auditing experience in A5 Grade in SLT

(b) Engineering Profession

(i) Corporate Membership of IESL/IET(IEB)UK/IMechE (UK)/Institution of Civil Engineers (UK) WITH 03 years' experience in A5 Grade in SLT OR
(ii) An Engineering Degree/ Associate Membership of IESL/IET(IEB)UK/IMechE (UK)/ Institution of Civil Engineers (UK) WITH 06 years' experience in A5 Grade in SLT OR
(iii) 08 years' experience in A5 Grade in SLT

2.4 Officers in A4 Grade who do not possess required number of experience in A4 Grade under category 2.2 will be eligible under category 2.3 by adding the number of years' experience in A5 Grade and A4 Grade together and those officers may also apply.

3 SELECTION METHOD

Selection will be by an interview.

4 PLACEMENT

(a) Transfer (As per Section 1)
Selected Deputy General Manager will be transferred as DGM-Employee Relations

(b) Promotion (As per Section 2)
Selected candidate will be initially appointed in Acting capacity. Subsequent to serving in the acting capacity for 06 months' period, if the selected officer is in A4 Grade, he /she will be placed in A3 Grade and if the selected officer is in A5 Grade he / she will be placed in A4 Grade subject to provisions available in SRPS. Further, if the selected officer in A3 Grade, his / her Grade will remain unchanged.
