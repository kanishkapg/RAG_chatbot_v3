To All Staff,

ORGANIZATIONAL REALIGNMENT

Please note that the reporting line of the Internal Control of Mobitel will be changed with immediate effect.

In line with the unified SLTMobitel Group organizational structure, the direct reporting line of the Internal Control Department of Mobitel will be changed from Chief Operating Officer of SLT to the Forensics & Compliance Group of SLTMobitel, with immediate effect.

Amended organizational structure of Forensics & Compliance Group of SLTMobitel is attached for your reference.

Janaka R Abeysinghe
Chief Executive Officer
