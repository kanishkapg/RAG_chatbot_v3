CEO’s Office, Sri Lanka Telecom PLC, Colombo 01.

30th June 2023.

To All Staff,

APPOINTMENT OF STAFF RE-ARRANGEMENT COMMITTEE (SRC)

SLT has been experiencing higher staff turnover among many professionals. In the recent past, it has been observed that the highly skilled graduates and professionals in some identified disciplines such as IT, software development, cloud & digital technologies/IDC, cyber security, network planning/operations, marketing and corporate/consumer sales have left the organization. Therefore it is vital to manage the situation which will allow the company to continue its operations without any difficulty. In order to overcome this situation, it is required to formulate new or alternative business/operation models to fill the critical staffing requirements.

Considering the situation, Management has decided to appoint a committee named Staff Re-arrangement Committee (SRC) to review the existing staff capabilities and propose alternative sourcing/staff re-arrangement methods to overcome the present issues and problems of staff shortage to suit the present business needs and meet the domestic & global market competition that SLT has to face in the short and long run. The committee is authorized to submit suitable recommendations to the Chief Executive Officer of SLT.
