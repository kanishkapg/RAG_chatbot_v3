Appointment of Environmental Social and Governance (ESG) Strategic Committee

SLT and MOBITEL are committed to conducting their businesses in a sustainable and responsible manner, considering environmental, social, and governance (ESG) factors in all aspects of their operations. To further strengthen this commitment, the companies are pleased to announce the establishment of an ESG Strategic Committee.

The ESG Strategic Committee will have the following key objectives:

- The committee will guide the development and implementation of strategies and practices that integrate ESG principles into the core business operations of both SLT and MOBITEL.

- The committee will oversee the companies' compliance with relevant ESG standards, regulations, and best practices.

- The committee will proactively identify and assess potential ESG risks and opportunities, and develop strategies to mitigate risks and capitalize on opportunities.

- The committee will recommend the establishment of necessary governance boards or frameworks to enhance transparency, accountability, and effective decision-making on ESG matters.
