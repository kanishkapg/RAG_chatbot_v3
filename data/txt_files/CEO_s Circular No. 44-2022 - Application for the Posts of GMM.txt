CEO's Circular No: 4/4/2022

CEO's Office, Sri Lanka Telecom PLC, Colombo 01.

T0: All General Managers & Deputy General Managers,

APPLICATIONS FOR THE POSTS OF:

1. GENERAL MANAGER – GOVERNMENT BUSINESS – (CEWO GROUP)
2. GENERAL MANAGER – ENTERPRISE PRODUCTS & DIGITAL SOLUTIONS (CEWO GROUP)
3. GENERAL MANAGER – BUSINESS SUPPORT & PROCESSES (CIDO GROUP)

Applications are invited from the permanent General Managers/ Deputy General Managers who satisfy the under mentioned requirements to be considered for the above posts.

1. ELIGIBILITY FOR TRANSFER

GMs (A1, A2 & A3) in Engineering profession who wish to be transferred to above positions shall apply.

2. ELIGIBILITY FOR PROMOTION

2.1 FOR DGMs PRESENTLY IN A2 GRADE:

DGMs (A2) in Engineering Profession who wish to be considered for the above positions shall apply.

2.2 FOR DGMs PRESENTLY IN A3 GRADE:

(a) Engineering Profession

(i) Corporate Membership of IESL/IET (IEE) UK/MechE (UK)/Institution of Civil Engineers (UK) with 5 years' experience in A3 Grade in SLT.

2.3 FOR DGMs PRESENTLY IN A4 GRADE:

(a) Engineering Profession

(i) Corporate Membership of IESL/IET (IEE) UK/ IMechE (UK)/Institution of Civil Engineers (UK) with 3 years engineering experience in A4 Grade in SLT.
OR
(ii) An Engineering Degree/Associate Membership of IESL/IET (IEE) UK/IMechE (UK)/Institution of Civil Engineers (UK) with 5 years engineering experience in A4 Grade in SLT.
OR
(iii) 7 years engineering experience in A4 Grade in SLT

CEO's Circular No: 4-4/2022

2.4 Deputy General Managers in A3 Grade who do not possess required number of years' experience in A3 Grade under category 2.2 will be eligible under category 2.3 by adding the number of years' experience in A4 Grade and A3 Grade together and those officers may also apply.

3 SELECTION METHOD
Selection will be by an interview.

4 PLACEMENT

(a) Transfer (As per Section 1)
Selected General Managers will be transferred to the relevant GM positions.

(b) Promotion (As per Section 2)

Selected candidates will be initially appointed in Acting capacity. Subsequent to serving in the acting capacity for 06 months' period, if the selected officer is in A3 Grade, he/she will be placed in A2 Grade and if the selected officer is in A4 Grade he/she will be placed in A3 Grade subject to provisions available in SRPS. Further, if the selected officer is in A2 Grade, his/her Grade will remain unchanged.

5 METHOD OF APPLYING

Eligible candidates should fill in the attached application form and forward to GM-HR & IR through the relevant Supervising Officer.

6 CLOSING DATE

Applications should be sent within 07 working days of issuing this Circular.
