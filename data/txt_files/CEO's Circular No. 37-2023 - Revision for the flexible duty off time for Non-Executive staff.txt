To All Employees,

Revision for the flexible duty off time for Non-Executive Staff

SLT Management is pleased to inform that the flexible duty “OFF time” has been revised for the Non-executive staff working in clerical & allied grades, technical grades and customer care assistant grades whose present duty time is 8.30 – 17.00 hrs, as per the following guidelines with effect from 01st June 2023.

a) If an employee reports to work before the assigned IN time (i.e. 8.30 hrs), he/she will be allowed to leave the office 30 Mins prior to the assigned OUT time (i.e. the assigned Out time is 17.00 hrs and the employee will be allowed to leave the office at 16.30 hrs) without disturbing daily assigned operational work.

b) If the said employee requests to perform overtime, the overtime calculation will start from 17.00 hrs onwards.

c) Above flexible duty off time is not applicable for following scenarios:

i. For employees who have obtained prior approval to perform pre-OT.
ii. For employees who have been assigned to shift operations.

d) Duty time of above employees will remain as 8.30 – 17.00 hrs for all other administrative purposes.

After the implementation of the above, the heads of respective functional units are required to ensure that work is not disturbed, and customer service is maintained as per the business/operational KPIs.
