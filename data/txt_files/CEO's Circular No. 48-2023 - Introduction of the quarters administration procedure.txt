To All Staff,

INTRODUCTION OF THE QUARTERS ADMINISTRATION PROCEDURE

Quarters Administration Procedure (Document No: HSPS/QAP/025) has been introduced to streamline the process of allocating SLT quarters to employees. The procedure formalizes allocation, stay and administration of the limited number of quarters available among the eligible employees based on business exigency.

The Quarters Administration Procedure of SLT PLC (Document No: HSPS/QAP/025) is available at SLT intranet [General>SLT Common Document Portal>Policies>Human Resources (CPO)>Quarters Administration Procedure].

The Quarter Administration Procedure shall come into effect on 15th July 2023 and shall supersedes all previous circulars, procedures, approvals and memos issued in this regard.

Janaka R. Abeysinghe Chief Executive Officer
