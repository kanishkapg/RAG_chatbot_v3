GCEO's Circular No: 01 /2022

GCEO's Office, Sri Lanka Telecom PLC, Colombo 01.

25th January 2022

To All Staff,

APPOINTMENT OF CHIEF EXECUTIVE OFFICER – SRI LANKA TELECOM PLC

Please be informed that Mr. K. M. J. R. Abeysinghe has been appointed as the Chief Executive Officer of Sri Lanka Telecom PLC with effect from 1st February 2022.

Mr. Abeysinghe joined SLT in 1991 and in his present role as the CEWO, he leads the Enterprise & Wholesale business of SLT that provides integrated voice and data solutions to enterprises, government institutions, domestic telco operators and global wholesale carriers.

In his career at SLT spanning 30 years, Mr. Abeysinghe has held a number of senior positions, including General Manager – Enterprise & Government Business, Head of Division – Corporate Sales I, Head of Section – Data Communications, Head of Section – Solution Business and has extensive experience in the areas of Enterprise Digital Services, Enterprise Communications Solutions, Data Communications, Business Development, Domestic & International Switching Operations and Global Wholesale Voice & Data Business.

He holds a Master's Degree in Electrical & Computer Engineering from the University of Kansas, USA and a BSc degree in Electronics & Telecommunications Engineering with First Class Honors from the University of Moratuwa, Sri Lanka.

Mr. Abeysinghe is a Fulbright Scholar, a Chartered Engineer, member of the Institution of Engineers Sri Lanka, and a member of the Computer Society of Sri Lanka.

We wish him all success in the new job role.

Lalith Seneviratne  
Group Chief Executive Officer
