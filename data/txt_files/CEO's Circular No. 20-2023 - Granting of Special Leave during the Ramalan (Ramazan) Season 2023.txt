CEO’s Circular No: 20/2023

CEO’s Office, Sri Lanka Telecom PLC Colombo 01.
14th March 2023

To All Executives,

Granting of Special Leave during the Ramalan (Ramazan) Season 2023

As the forthcoming Ramalan (Ramazan) Season is due to commence on 23rd March 2023 and end on 21st April 2023, kindly make arrangements to enable Muslim employees to take part in prayers and religious activities during this period without disturbing the normal duties.

This special leave can be granted for one and a half hours period for a day either in the morning or afternoon as they wish.

Janaka R. Abeysinghe
Chief Executive Officer
