To All Staff,

APPOINTMENT OF DCFO/SPECIAL PROJECTS

Please be informed that Mr. S P E Wijesuriya (GM/Treasury) is appointed for the position of DCFO/Special Projects with immediate effect.

After successful completion of work assigned during the initial period of six months as DCFO/Special Projects, Mr. Wijesuriya will be placed on the position of Chief Financial Officer (CFO) of SLT PLC reporting to Group CFO.

Further, Mr. Wijesuriya will coverup the duties of GM/Treasury position until further notice.

We wish him all success in the new job role.

Janaka R. Abeysinghe
Chief Executive Officer
