CEO's Circular No: 03/2024

CEO's Office, Sri Lanka Telecom PLC, Colombo 01.
05th January 2024

To All Non-Executive Staff,

PAYMENT OF A SALARY ADVANCE FOR ALL NON-EXECUTIVE STAFF AS A HARDSHIP MITIGATION CONCESSION

SLTMobitel Board has approved to disburse a salary advance to all non-executive staff of SLTMobitel Group, who have applied to obtain a salary advance as described by the below clause.

A salary advance of up to two (02) months of basic salary, subject to a maximum of LKR 200,000/- will be paid on request to any non-executive employee of SLTMobitel Group who is eligible and has applied for such an advance. This salary advance will be fully recovered by the company by deducting from employees salary after a 3 month grace period (from April 2024 onwards) in equal 9 monthly instalments.

Therefore, item (b) of the CEO's Circular No. 98/2023 dated 21st December 2023, on "Hardship Mitigation Concession for the Non-executive Staff," is replaced with the above clause.

Please note that a payment of LKR 35,500/- has already been made to all non-executive staff of SLTMobitel Group as a hardship mitigation concession in December 2023.

The deadline for application for the salary advance is January 15, 2024.

Eligible employees who wish to apply for this salary advance shall apply for the same via the path given in the ERP system.

Direct application for employees

SLT Employee Self-Service -> Loan -> Cash advance Request

Application through Leave Agents

SLT OTL Timekeeper -> Cash Advance Request

Janaka R Abeysinghe
