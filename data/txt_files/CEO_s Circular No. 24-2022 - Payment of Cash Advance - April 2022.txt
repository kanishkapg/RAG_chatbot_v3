CEO's Circular No. 24/2022

CEO's Office, Sri Lanka Telecom PLC, Colombo 01.

31st March 2022

To: All SLT Staff,

PAYMENT OF CASH ADVANCE - APRIL 2022

SLT management is pleased to acknowledge and recognize your determination, passion and commitment to manage our services while providing the best experience to our customers.

I am pleased to inform that the Board of Directors has approved a cash advance payment of One (01) month's basic salary in April 2022 subject to a minimum of LKR 70,000/- as an advance bonus payment to all SLT permanent staff and employees who were recruited on a contract basis from HCS to SLT (under the intake of June 2020). This cash advance will be deducted from the year-end annual bonus, which will be decided based on the performance of the company and individual employee performance. This cash advance will be paid during the first week of April 2022 in complying with the other terms and conditions stipulated in related policy documents of the company.

I take this opportunity to thank all of you for your significant contribution and dedication during this year. I am confident that with your continuous support and by working together, keeping focused on our priorities and putting our customers first, we can realize organizational corporate objectives in the year 2022.

I wish all SLT employees and members of their families a happy and prosperous Sinhala and Hindu New Year.

Janaka R. Abeysinghe Chief Executive Officer
