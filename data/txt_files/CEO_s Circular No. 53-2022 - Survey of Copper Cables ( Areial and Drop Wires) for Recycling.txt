Survey of Copper Cables (Aerial Cables & Drop wires) for Recycling

This circular is issued to facilitate the survey of Copper Aerial Cables and Copper Drop Wires for recycling.

1) A Board of Survey (BOS) shall be appointed to conduct the survey for identifying the Aerial Copper Cables and Copper Drop Wires for recycling. The process chart is shown in Annexure 1
2) Composition of the BOS will be as follows;
    ➢ Relevant Provincial DGM - Chairman
    ➢ Relevant OPMC Manager - Secretary
    ➢ Regional Accountant – Member
    ➢ Any Executive Officer(s) from the relevant Region (other than OPMC & Regional Accountant's Office) – Member
3) BOS Chairman will obtain recommendation of Regional GM and approval of the CAO for the appointment of the BOS and inform the members of BOS through email.

a) List of attachments
    ➢ Process chart for survey of Aerial Copper Cables and Copper Drop Wires for recycling (Annexure 1)
b) List of forms
    ➢ List of Copper Cables to be recycled (CAOO/FRM/001)
    ➢ Approval for disposal of copper cables through recycling (CAOO/FRM/002)

All forms related to this circular can be downloaded from the intranet at the following location:

General-SLT COMMON DOCUMENT Portal>Forms>Administration (CAO)

Note:

I. Please refer Process Guide on "Recycle of Abandoned Copper Cables" (Document No OSD GRM 03) for additional information on copper recycling  
II. If Aerial Copper Cables and Drop wires are sold without recycling, approval should be obtained from CAO prior to the sale  
III. This circular excludes all Underground Cables identified for disposal
