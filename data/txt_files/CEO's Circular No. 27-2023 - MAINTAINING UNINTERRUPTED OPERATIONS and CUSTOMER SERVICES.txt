CEO’s Office,
Sri Lanka Telecom PLC,
Colombo 01

1st April 2023

**To All Staff,**

**MAINTAINING UNINTERRUPTED OPERATIONS & CUSTOMER SERVICES**

Further to the **CEO’s Circular No: 89 / 2022 (Annexure 1), issued on 07[th] December 2022 on the same**
**subject, attention of all employees is drawn towards cooperating and complying with the instructions of**
**their superiors to maintain uninterrupted operations and customer services. SLT’s operation is essential**
for the continuation of the critical services and business activities in the country. Therefore, it is the
responsibility of all employees to discharge their assigned business/operational duties without an interruption
by adhering to the assigned mode of work. Any interruption to the operations of the company and the customer
service will have adverse effects on the future of the company.

The management has already made all the measures to continue the business and operations of the company in
the usual manner without any disruption. Further, all the Group Chief Officers, Chief Officers, the
**Regional, Divisional and the Sectional management are empowered to take the business and operational**
**decisions to ensure business continuity of the company.**

**The management seeks cooperation of all staff to refrain from engaging in any activity which is**
**detrimental to the interests of the company and to report to their workstations and engage in the**
**assigned duties as directed by their immediate supervisors to maintain uninterrupted operations and**
**customer services.**

**Janaka R Abeysinghe**
**Chief Executive Officer**
