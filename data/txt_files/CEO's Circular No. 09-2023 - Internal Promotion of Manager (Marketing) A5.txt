CEO’s Office, Sri Lanka Telecom PLC, Colombo 01.

To: All Executive Staff,

INTERNAL PROMOTION OF MANAGER (MARKETING) AS
Applications are invited from the permanent, confirmed SLT employees who satisfy the under mentioned requirements to be considered for the vacancies stated below;

1. Manager (Marketing) - Brand Marketing & Research
2. Manager (Marketing) - Public Relations
3. Manager (Marketing) - Global Account Management
4. Manager (Marketing) - SME Product Development & Management

A. ELIGIBILITY
Three (03) years’ experience in A6 Grade as a Senior Executive Assistant Manager (Marketing) A6 in SLT as at 31st January 2023

B. ELIGIBILITY FOR TRANSFER
Manager Marketing in A3, A4 and A5 Grades in Marketing Profession who wish to be transferred to the above positions shall apply.

C. SELECTION
1. Selection will be made through an examination and an interview for promotion. Selection will be made from an interview for Transfer.
2. Examination will be held in English Medium.
3. Interview will be held only for successful candidates of the examination for the final selection.

The subjects specified for the examination are as follows:
i) SLT Procedures, Practices & Business Affairs (40 minutes)
ii) Subject Knowledge
- Part I - Multiple Choice Questions (45 minutes)
- Part II - Essay Type Questions (1 ¼ hours)

D. PLACEMENT
(a) Promotion (As per Section A)
Selected candidate will be promoted as Manager Marketing A5 to the selected position

(b) Transfer (As per Section B)
Selection will be done by an Interview and selected Manager Marketing will be transferred for the requested position.

E. METHOD OF APPLYING
Eligible candidates should fill in the attached application form and forward to DGM/ HR Operations, 7th Floor, HQ Building, Sri Lanka Telecom PLC, Lotus Road, Colombo 01 through the relevant General Manager / Deputy General Manager.

F. CLOSING DATE
Applications should be sent within seven (07) working days of issuing this circular. Applications sent after the closing date will not be considered.

Janaka R Abeysinghe
Chief Executive Officer
