CEO’s Circular No: 10/2023

CEO’s Office, Sri Lanka Telecom PLC, Colombo 01.

17th February 2023

To All SLT Staff,

Internal Recruitment of Manager Marketing (A5)

Applications are invited from permanent & confirmed SLT employees who possess the following qualifications for the vacancies stated below.

1. Manager (Marketing) - Brand Marketing & Research.
2. Manager (Marketing) - Public Relations.
3. Manager (Marketing) - Global Account Management.
4. Manager (Marketing) - SME Product Development & Management.

1. Eligibility Criteria & Qualifications.

a. Special Degree in Marketing or Postgraduate Diploma in Marketing from SLIM or Professional Postgraduate Diploma in Marketing from CIM-UK. AND
b. Two years post qualifying experience in Marketing/Sales in SLT as at 31st January 2023.

Note: Applicants who are having one year post qualifying experience in Marketing/Sales in SLT and additional two years’ work experience in Marketing/Sales in SLT as at 31st January 2023 in lieu of rest of the one year post qualifying experience may also apply (This condition is applicable only for this Circular).

2. Method of Selection.

Selection will be made through a written examination (English Medium) and interviews.

3. Method of Applying:

Application should be submitted through ERP System to reach HR Strategies & Policy Section within 14 days of issuing this circular. Scan copies of relevant certificates should be attached.

ERP >SLT Employee Self-Service>Other Requests>Internal Recruitments>Click on ‘Create Application’>Select the Post Applied>Specify the ‘Vacancy Position/s Applied> Attach Relevant Certificates>Add Qualifications>Click on ‘Create’>Click on ‘Application Number’>tick the ‘Declaration’>Submit. (Application will be sent to A5 or above supervisor for recommendation and to the HR thereafter).

Applications sent without supporting documents will not be entertained. Correspondence will only be with the shortlisted applicants. Relevant Job Descriptions can be obtained from Randima in HR (0714752278/randimap@slt.com.lk).

Janaka R. Abeysinghe Chief Executive Officer
