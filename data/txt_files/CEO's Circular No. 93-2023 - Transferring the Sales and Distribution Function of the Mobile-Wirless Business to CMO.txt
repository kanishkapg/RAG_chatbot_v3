CEO’s Circular No: 93/2023

CEO’s Office, Sri Lanka Telecom PLC, Colombo 01.

14th December 2023

To All Staff,

Transferring the Sales & Distribution Function of the Mobile/Wireless Business to CMO

Please be informed that the responsibilities of the Sales & Distribution function of the Wireless Business (Mobile & fixed LTE) will be assigned to Mr. Prabhath Dahanayake (CMO) with immediate effect in addition to his present Group marketing functions.

CMO will report to COO-Mobitel for businesses relating to wireless business. Accordingly, Mr. Sugath Abeysinghe (GM – Consumer Sales & Distribution) and Mr. Rukshan De Silva (GM - New Market Development) will report to CMO.

Further, Mr. Imantha Wijekoon (CBO-CB) shall be responsible for the Sales function of SLT fixed wired business (FTTH & IPTV) and the Fixed LTE business will operate under CBO-CB as a Sales Channel for the Wireless Products under CMO.

Janaka R Abeysinghe Chief Executive Officer
