**Intrapreneurship Studio Programme – Cohort 2023/24**
**Invitation for Continuous Research and Development Engagements**

While acknowledging your continuous active engagement with the Research and Development team, we are pleased to announce that SLT-MOBITEL management have planned to introduce a rewarding and recognition scheme with a Credit Value Coin System and all innovators including development teams will be rewarded financially and they will be recognized at promotions, performance appraisals, transfers, and training opportunities. The system will be launching soon.

With the establishment of “The Embryo” group R&D center, we are delighted to see your continuous engagement in innovations, where employees interested in researching & innovation activities are invited to join hands with “The Embryo” Group R&D center. Opportunities are open for all enthusiastic staff members to engage with the Group R&D center to move forward with their new ideas and we will facilitate them to groom as innovators.
