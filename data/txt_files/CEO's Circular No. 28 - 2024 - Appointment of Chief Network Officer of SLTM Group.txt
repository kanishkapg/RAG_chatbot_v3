To All Staff,

APPOINTMENT OF CHIEF NETWORK OFFICER OF SLTMOBITEL GROUP

Please be informed that Mr. M S Samarasinha has been appointed to the position of Chief Network Officer (CNO) of SLTMobitel Group with immediate effect.

Mr. Samarasinha will report to the Chief Executive Officer.

Further, Mr. Samarasinha will overlook the duties of Deputy Chief Officer – Product Management until a suitable replacement is made.

Mr. M S Samarasinha joined SLT as an Engineer in 1992 and has served in the capacity of Engineer Transmission Planning, Engineer Switching Planning, Deputy General Manager, General Manager, Deputy Chief Enterprise & Wholesale Officer, Deputy Chief Officer – Product Management, before being appointed as the Chief Network Officer of SLTMobitel Group.

He holds a B.Sc (Eng.) degree in Electronics & Telecommunications Engineering from the University of Moratuwa, Sri Lanka. He is a Chartered Engineer and is a Corporate Member of the Institution of Engineers, Sri Lanka.

We wish him all success in his new job role.

Janaka R Abeysinghe
Chief Executive Officer
