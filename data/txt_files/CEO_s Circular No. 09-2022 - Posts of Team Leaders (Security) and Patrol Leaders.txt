CEO's Circular No: 09/2022

CEO's Office, Sri Lanka Telecom PLC, Colombo 01.

To All Security Staff,

POSTS OF TEAM LEADERS (SECURITY) AND PATROL LEADERS

Applications are invited from the permanent, confirmed SLT Security Staff who satisfy the under mentioned requirements to be considered for the above mentioned positions.

A. ELIGIBILITY

Team Leaders (Security) - S4

• 10 years satisfactory service as a Patrolman in the S5 salary grade at SLT as at 31st January 2022
AND
Should have followed at least 05 prescribed training courses conducted by SLT Security Division whilst service as Patrolman

Patrol Leaders - S3

• 06 years satisfactory service as a Team Leader (Security) in the S4 salary grade at SLT as at 31st January 2022
AND
Should have followed at least 03 prescribed training courses conducted by SLT Security Division whilst service as Team Leader (Security)

B. SELECTION METHOD

Selection will be made through an interview.

C. PLACEMENT

Selected candidates will be promoted as Team Leader (Security) - S4 / Patrol Leader – S3 and placed under vacant positions

D. METHOD OF APPLYING

Eligible candidates should fill in the attached application form and forward to DGM/HR Operations, 7th Floor, HQ Building, Sri Lanka Telecom PLC, Lotus Road, Colombo 01 through the relevant General Manager / Deputy General Manager.

E. CLOSING DATE

Applications should be sent within seven (07) working days of issuing this circular. Applications sent after the closing date will not be considered.

Janaka R Abeysinghe
Chief Executive Officer
