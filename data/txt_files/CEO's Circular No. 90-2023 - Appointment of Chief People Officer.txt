CEO’s Circular No: 90/2023

CEO’s Office, Sri Lanka Telecom PLC, Colombo 01
01st December 2023

To All Staff,

APPOINTMENT OF CHIEF PEOPLE OFFICER

Please be informed that Mr. Chandana Wijayanama has been appointed as the Chief People Officer of SLTMobitel Group with immediate effect.

Mr. Wijayanama will report to the Chief Executive Officer.

Mr. Wijayanama is a professional with over 25 years of extensive experience in HR and IT. Prior to joining SLT, he has worked as the Organization & HR Director – Siam City Cement (Lanka) Ltd, General Manager HR – South Asia Gateway Terminals Ltd, Regional Director HR (Based in Hong Kong) – Franke Food Service Systems Asia and Head of University Relations (Based in Zurich) – Holcim Group Support Ltd. Further he has extensive experience as SAP HR Project Manager, APAC (Based in Bangkok, Thailand), Holcim Group Support Ltd, Thailand, Manager Information Systems, Holcim (Lanka) Ltd. Manager Network Administration Lanka Bell Ltd and Systems Engineer, Informatics (Pvt) Ltd.

He holds a Doctor of Business Administration degree from the University of Kelaniya. Further he has completed Executive Education Programs in IMD – Switzerland, INSEAD – Singapore and at London Business School –UK and an Academic Program in Griffith University-Australia. Additionally, he holds a National Diploma in HRM – from the Chartered Institute of Personnel Management, Sri Lanka and a Master’s Degree in Computer Engineering & Networks from National University of Lviv Polytechnic, Ukraine.

We welcome Mr. Chandana Wijayanama to SLTMobitel Group and wish him all success in his new job role.

Janaka R Abeysinghe
Chief Executive Officer
