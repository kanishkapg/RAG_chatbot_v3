To All Employees,

Update on the Members of the Financial Advisory Committee (FAC)

The composition of the Financial Advisory Committee (FAC) is being updated. Accordingly, the CEO's Circular No. 11/2023 dated 20th February 2023 regarding the establishment of the Financial Advisory Committee (FAC) will be amended to incorporate the newly appointed members.

The new members of the committee are as follows:

1. Mr. Indika Gunawardana - Deputy Chief People Officer & GM-Human Capital Management (Chairman)
2. Mr. Indika Peiris - General Manager-Fixed Assets & Budget
3. Ms. Harshini Wijeratne - Deputy General Manager - Legal.
4. Mr. Asanga Wimalaratne - General Manager-Revenue Collection
5. Mr. Kapila Kulasena - Deputy General Manager-HR Operations
6. Ms. Shiromi Saputantri - Deputy General Manager-Employee & Industrial Relations

Interested employees are invited to forward their requests via email: ssaputantri@slt.com.lk .

Janaka R Abeysinghe
Chief Executive Officer
