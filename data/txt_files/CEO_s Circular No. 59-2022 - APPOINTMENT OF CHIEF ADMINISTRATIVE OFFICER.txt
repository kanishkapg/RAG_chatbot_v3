CEO's Circular No: 59 / 2022

CEO's Office, Sri Lanka Telecom PLC, Colombo 01
16th August 2022

To All Staff,

APPOINTMENT OF CHIEF ADMINISTRATIVE OFFICER

Please be informed that Mr. R M S K Piyarathna is appointed as Chief Administrative Officer (CAO) of Sri Lanka Telecom PLC with immediate effect.

Mr. Piyarathna will be reporting to Chief Operating Officer of SLT PLC.

Further, Mr. Piyarathna will overlook the duties of GM - Tender Evaluation until a suitable replacement is made.

Mr. R M S K Piyarathna joined SLT as an Engineer in 1994 and has served in the capacity of Engineer - Transmission, Regional Telecommunication Manager, Provincial Deputy General Manager, Regional General Manager, Deputy Chief Innovation & Culture Officer, Deputy Chief Commercial Evaluation Officer, Deputy Chief Supply Chain Management Officer before being appointed as the Chief Administrative Officer of SLT PLC.

He holds a BSc (Eng) in Electronics & Electrical Engineering and MBA from University of Peradeniya, Sri Lanka. He is a Chartered Engineer and is a Corporate Member of The Institution of Engineers, Sri Lanka.

We wish him all success in his new job role.

Janaka R Abeysinghe  
Chief Executive Officer
