To All Employees,

Prevention of Spreading of Dengue in SLT Group premises

As you are aware, dengue mosquito breeding has increased immensely all over the country. During the coming rainy season the situation may become worse.

Therefore, all GMM/DGMM especially in regions and in other respective areas and other staff are hereby requested to be vigilant and take immediate actions to prevent your own work premises and surroundings from mosquito breeding.

All those who occupy SLT Quarters too are responsible for managing their Quarters' premises in a clean and tidy manner to avoid mosquito breeding.

You all are requested to observe following areas through cleaning supervisors and health & safety officers and take immediate actions as follows to control dengue mosquito breeding:
- Keep all work and outside premises clean
- Clean the insides of flower vases, and plant pots and change water once a week
- Clear blockages and clean drains and gutters once a week
- Dispose of any unused containers and objects that can accumulate water
- Turn over any containers that cannot be thrown away and protect them from accumulating water

Instructions for Officers occupying SLT Quarters
✓ Surrounding area of the premises/Quarters should be kept clean and free of containers and objects that can accumulate water.
✓ Clear blockages and clean drains and gutters regularly (at least once a week)
✓ Any unattainable cases to be reported to relevant authority of the Quarters management and the matter to be followed up until the issue is rectified
✓ Current user of the Quarters is responsible for any health-related issues/hazards that arise in the premises/environment.

Please refer Clause B9 of the the Health & Safety Manual for more details to protect your premises from dengue breeding.
