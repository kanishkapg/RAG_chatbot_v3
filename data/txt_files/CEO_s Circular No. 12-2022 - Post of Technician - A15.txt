CEO's Circular No: /2 /2022

CEO's Office, Sri Lanka Telecom PLC, Colombo 01.

To All SLT Staff,

Post of Technician-A15

Employees who are qualified with NVQ Level 4 get an opportunity to apply for the posts of Technician A15 under this special opportunity scheme. Accordingly, applications are invited from permanent SLT employees who possess following qualifications and fulfill other eligibility criteria as at 28th February 2022 for the post of Technician A15.

1. Qualifications & Eligibility Criteria;

a) A16 or A15 grade permanent employee with satisfactory service records.
    AND
b) 06 passes at the G.C.E. (O/L) exam including Sinhala/Tamil, English Language and Mathematics and 03 credit passes in one sitting.
    AND
c) Possess NVQ 4 or equivalent certification in the field of Telecommunication / Electrical /IT/ Power /AC or any other
