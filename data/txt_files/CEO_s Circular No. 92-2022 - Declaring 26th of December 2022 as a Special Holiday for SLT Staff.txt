DECLARING 26TH OF DECEMBER 2022 AS A SPECIAL HOLIDAY FOR SLT STAFF  

SLT Management has decided to declare 26th December 2022 as a special holiday for all SLT employees.  

However, the employees who have already been assigned operational/essential duties on the above day should report to work and the relevant payments due to them will be made accordingly.
