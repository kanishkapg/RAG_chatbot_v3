ESTABLISHING INDIVIDUAL TARGETS VIA ERP SYSTEM FOR THE YEAR – 2023

Please be informed that the establishing individual targets for the year 2023 should be done via ERP system with immediate effect. Effective duration for the evaluation is from 01st January 2023 to 31st December 2023. Establishing individual targets is a significant supervisory responsibility, because it directly enables staff members to perform as effectively as possible to support sectional/divisional and company performance.

Please note that all supervising officers are responsible on ensuring establishing individual KPIs for their subordinates in line with the company ABP before the deadline. Accordingly, duly completed KPIs should be updated in the ERP system by relevant immediate supervisor and cascaded to subordinates after proper approval.

Establishing Individual KPIs.

Individual KPIs of all SLT staff should be set-up through ERP system using the ‘2023 SLT Performance Management Plan’.

Nominating Supervisor’s Peers for performance appraisal.

Relevant supervising officer should nominate two peers for each employee for the purpose of year end performance appraisal. Relevant employee and the peers should be clearly communicated about the nomination and same should be updated in the ERP system as well. Supervisor’s peers are usually officers at the same level or rank work with the supervisor. Peers can be appointed within the group or outside the group. Accordingly peer may comprise of employees from same/different divisions/sections or teams that are working together as one unit for the completion of a project/target.

Guidelines

All executive supervising officers are requested to educate their subordinates on the KPI setting procedure and to facilitate the process via ERP.

The following directions will guide you in establishing, achieving and monitoring targets & objectives for your staff:

- All Chief Officers are already established individual targets and objectives for their subordinates in line with Group/Divisional operational plan based on the company Annual Business Plan (ABP). Accordingly Group’s ABP targets should be cascaded into each General Manager, Deputy General Manager and all staff reporting below them.

- Key Results Areas (KRAs)/Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) recommended by the Management/ABP for each group shall be taken into consideration when establishing individual KPIs.

- All supervising officers are responsible to establish KPIs to all their subordinates. Supervising officers also should ensure update their subordinates’ KPIs and assignment of supervisor peers in ERP system.

- CEO and Group Chief Officers will peer audit DGM and above officers to ensure cross functional alignment of ABP objectives with operational plans and staff responsibilities on KPIs.

- Targets/KPIs must be defined by considering the short term and long term goals of the group and they should be defined based on the targets ABP 2023 and respective divisional & sectional operational plans should be linked to ABP 2023 targets.

- Individual targets/KPIs have to be SMART and linked to operational plan targets.
  - Specific (no ambiguity)
  - Measurable (in terms of time, number, volume or value)
  - Agreed (mutually agreed)
  - Realistic (can be particularly attainable, but only with an extra effort)
  - Time- bound (must have a time schedule)

- They should be defined as appropriate for each employee under the following key areas.
  - Customer
  - Financial
  - Internal Business Processes
  - Learning & Growth

- Achievement of individual KPIs should be monitored monthly by all reporting officers. Employees should discuss their KPI targets and progress with their reporting officers regularly (at least every month). These documents should be made available for management reference at any time.

- Reviewing Officers should monitor the progress of performance throughout the year. Progress and deviations (if any) should be monitored continuously and corrective measures should be taken with the help of respective reporting officers.

- Employees are not permitted to add, modify or omit targets/KPIs once they are approved by the management. Any changes should be done with instructions/guidance and proper approvals of the respective supervising officer and the reviewer.

- Management has revised the Quantitative Rating Scale for the evaluation of Individual KPJ/Targets, Competencies/Soft Skills and Final Performance Rating of the employees, new rating scale is as follows.

| Achievement (X) out of 100 % | Reference Rating | Definition    |
|---|---|---|
| X > 100%    | 5    | Outstanding    |
| 85% ≤ X ≤ 100%    | 4    | Superior    |
| 70% ≤ X < 85%    | 3    | Satisfactory    |
| 50% ≤ X < 70%    | 2    | Need Improvements    |
| X < 50%    | 1    | Unsatisfactory    |
