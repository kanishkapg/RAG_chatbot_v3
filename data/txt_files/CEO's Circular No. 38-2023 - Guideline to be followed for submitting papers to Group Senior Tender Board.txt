GUIDLELINES TO BE FOLLOWED FOR SUBMITTING PAPERS TO GROUP SENIOR TENDER BOARD

Following guidelines with respect to Group Senior Tender Board (GSTB) submissions are required to be strictly followed when submitting papers for GSTB approval.

1.) Business owners should ensure that the Investment Governance Process is followed and SIMC approval is obtained

2.) For investments/projects where SIMC had granted approval prior to 31st December 2022, Business owners should get the business case reviewed, revalidated by the relevant IMC and approved by the SIMC

3.) Obtain GCFO endorsement on the GSTB submission confirming the funds availability. Only the submissions with confirmed funds shall be considered at the Pre-STB.

4.) It is essential to comply with the GSTB submission deadlines and only GSTB submissions, including presentations reviewed and accepted at the Pre-STB shall be submitted to GSTB.

5.) Any corrections to the submissions, if directed by the Pre-STB should be made and resubmitted to the GM/SCM within one day with the GCFO endorsement and the signatures of the COO and CEO
