CEO's Office

08th April 2022.

To All Staff,

SPECIAL WORK ARRANGEMENTS ON 11th & 12th OF APRIL 2022

Please be informed that 11th and 12th April 2022 are considered to be normal working days and the following special work arrangements will be implemented across SLT PLC.

a. Work From Home (WFH) arrangements will be implemented, where possible subject to the approval of relevant CXO, GMM and DGMM.

b. Home to Field (H2F) arrangements will be implemented to manage all Regional OPMC and Network Operations where deemed necessary.

c. All Regional Telecom Offices (RTOs) and Teleshops (TS) will be opened for public to maintain Bill Collection and Customer Service activities. All Regional Management are advised to arrange required staff to man payments and customer service counters in RTO & TS on above dates on a roster basis.

d. All shift operations will be continued in usual manner as per pre-scheduled rosters as a working day.

However, the employees who have already been assigned to operational/essential duties on above days should report for work as usual.

Janaka R Abeysinghe
Chief Executive Officer
