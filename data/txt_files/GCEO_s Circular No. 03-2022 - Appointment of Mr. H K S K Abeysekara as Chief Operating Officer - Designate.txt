GCEO's Circular No: 03 /2022

GCEO's Office, Sri Lanka Telecom PLC, Colombo 01.

02nd February 2022

To All Staff,

APPOINTMENT OF MR. H K S K ABEYSEKARA AS CHIEF OPERATING OFFICER – DESIGNATE

Please be informed that Mr. H K S K Abeysekara has been appointed to the position of Chief Operating Officer (COO) – Designate of SLT PLC. Mr Abeysekara will assume duties as COO of SLT PLC upon retirement of the current COO Mr M B P Fernandez in July 2022.

Meanwhile, Mr Fernandez will be reporting the GCEO until his retirement.

Mr Abeysekara will continue to function in the CAO position while being the understudy to the COO.

Mr Abeysekara joined SLT in 1998 and has served for SLT in the capacity of Regional Telecommunication Manager, Provincial Deputy General Manager, Regional General Manager and Deputy Chief Regional Operating Officer, Chief Asset Property and Security Management Officer before being appointed as the Chief Administrative Officer of SLT as his present role.

He holds a BSc (Eng.) in Electrical and Electronics Engineering from University of Peradeniya, Sri Lanka and is a Chartered Engineer and a Fellow of the Institution of Engineers Sri Lanka.

We wish him all success in the new job role.

Lalith Seneviratne  
Group Chief Executive Officer
