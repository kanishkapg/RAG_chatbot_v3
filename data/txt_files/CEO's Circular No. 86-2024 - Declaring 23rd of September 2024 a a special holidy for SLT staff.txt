CEO's Office
Sri Lanka Telecom PLC,

22nd September 2024.

To All Staff,

DECLARING 23rd OF SEPTEMBER 2024 AS A SPECIAL HOLIDAY FOR SLT STAFF

SLT Management has decided to declare Monday, the 23rd of September 2024 as a Special Holiday for all SLT employees.

However, the employees who have already been assigned operational/essential duties on the 23rd of September 2024, shall report to work and the relevant payments due to them will be made accordingly.

Janaka R Abeysinghe
Chief Executive Officer
