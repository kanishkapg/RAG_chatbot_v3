CEO’s Circular No: 09/2024

CEO’s Office, Sri Lanka Telecom PLC, Colombo 01.
17th January 2024

To All Staff,

ORGANIZATION REALIGNMENT

Considering the operational requirements, following organization re-alignment has been made with immediate effect.

| Name                | Present Designation               | New Position Designation          | Reports to       |
|---------------------|-----------------------------------|-----------------------------------|------------------|
| Mr. R. L. Ashton    | DGM/Access Network Projects       | DGM/Network Infrastructure Projects | GM/Projects      |
| Mr. W.G.G. Gunathunga | DGM/UR & Projects Quality Assurance | DGM/Access Network Projects       | GM/National Fiberization Program & FTTH |

Accordingly, UR & Project Quality Assurance Section will be abolished and a new section Network Infrastructure Projects section is established. The job responsibilities and the staff attached to the UR & Project Quality Assurance Section will be re-distributed among Network Infrastructure Projects Section and Access Network Projects Sections.

Janaka R Abeysinghe
Chief Executive Officer
