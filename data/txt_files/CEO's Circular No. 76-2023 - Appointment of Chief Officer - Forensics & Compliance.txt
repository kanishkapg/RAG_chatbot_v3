CEO’s Office, Sri Lanka Telecom PLC, Colombo 01

To All Staff,

APPOINTMENT OF CHIEF OFFICER – FORENSICS & COMPLIANCE

Please be informed that Mr. Karuppiah Jegatheshwaran has been appointed as the Chief Officer – Forensics & Compliance of SLTMobitel with immediate effect.

Mr. Jegatheshwaran will report to the Board Audit Committee for all Forensics, Investigations & Compliance related functions and to the Chief Executive Officer for the Administrative related functions.

Mr. Jegatheshwaran is a professional with over 20 years of extensive experience in Risk & Control, Internal Audit, and Forensics across diverse industries. Prior to joining SLT, he has worked as the Senior Manager Forensics (EY- Vietnam), Director- Libras Consulting (Pvt) Ltd, Director (Forensic and Risk Advisory) - BDO Partners- Sri Lanka, Manager -Special Affairs (Group Internal audit) at Dialog Axiata PLC and Manager Forensic Services at KPMG in Sri Lanka and India.

He holds a Master of Business Administration (MBA) from Anglia Ruskin University in the UK. Furthermore, he is a Certified Member of the Association of Certified Fraud Examiners (ACFE), a Certified Member of the International Institute of Forensic Investigation Professionals (IICFIP) and the Country Representative for International Institute of Forensic Investigation Professionals (IICFIP).

We welcome Mr. Karuppiah Jegatheshwaran to SLTMobitel and wish him all success in his new job role.

Janaka R Abeysinghe
Chief Executive Officer
