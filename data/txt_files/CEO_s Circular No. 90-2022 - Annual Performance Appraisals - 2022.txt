**ANNUAL PERFORMANCE APPRAISAL-2022**

Please be informed that the annual performance appraisals for the year 2022 can be started with effective from 15.12.2022 and the effective duration for the evaluation is from 01st January 2022 to 31st December 2022. Managing staff performance is a significant supervisory responsibility, because it directly enables staff members to perform as effectively as possible to support divisional and company performance. Please note that all supervising officers are responsible to ensure completion of performance appraisal as per the appraisal time plan for all their subordinates and also need to complete their subordinates' appraisal before participating his/her own appraisal interview.

**Appraisal Process**
Appraisal process consists of 4 steps, namely self-appraisal (appraisee), supervisor appraisal (main appraiser), supervisor's peer appraisal (2 peers) and reviewer appraisal. Appraisal should be done via ERP system and approval process should be completed through relevant protocol with the approval of respective Chief Officer on or before the date specified under the time plan below.

**Appraisal Method**
Appraisals of all staff should be carried out through the ERP system. All supervising officers are responsible to ensure completion of performance appraisal in the ERP system for all their subordinates and also need to complete their subordinates' appraisal before participating his/her own appraisal interview. Further the respective supervising officer shall facilitate the process by assisting the staff who do not have dedicated computer to complete the appraisal in the ERP system throughout the performance appraisal process.

**Supervisor's Peer Appraisal (Peer of immediate supervisor)**
In view of enhancing the performance management process, the management introduced Supervisor's Peer appraisal from the year 2021 onward. Supervisor's peer is usually officer at the same level or rank work with the supervisor. Supervisor shall name two peers for each employee for performance appraisal, same should be updated in the ERP by relevant supervisor to initiate the appraisal process. Peers can be appointed within the group or outside the group. Accordingly peer may comprise of employees from same/different divisions/sections or teams that are working together as one unit for the completion of a project/target.

**Appraisal Interview**
It is compulsory to conduct year-end performance appraisal interview between appraisee, main appraiser, supervisor's peers and reviewer which will enable them to communicate first-hand information which will form part of their overall assessment. This interview should be conducted for each individual for a duration of at least 10 minutes. During the interview all the parties may share their views and feedback on level of targets achievements, competencies, gaps and areas to be improved during next year. Executive supervisory officers are held responsible for detail and record the competency gaps that should be developed by the appraisee, to support them, and to follow up actions on what employees should be accomplish through Training & Development.
It is compulsory to conduct performance appraisal interview and communicate relevant appraisal rating to the employee with feedback. Reviewer has to ensure completion of year-end performance appraisal interview before his/her evaluation.

Performance Based Training
Training plays a key role to achieve the ultimate objective of developing employees' performance. Right training guides the employees to improve their competencies and provide means of achieving the desirable level in performance. Executive supervising officers shall record and recommend training for their subordinates via ERP after completion of the appraisal. Accordingly all executive supervisory officers are held responsible to clearly identify the competency gaps that should be developed by their subordinates, direct them for required Training & Development, support them and follow up actions with adequate feedback.

Appraisal Time Plan
I. Completion of self-appraisal between 15th December 2022 - 20th December 2022.
II. Completion of main appraiser-appraisal before 25th December 2022.
III. Completion of supervisor's Peer-appraisal before 28th December 2022.
IV. Completion of reviewer-appraisal on or before 10th January 2023.
V. Completion of approval by the relevant Manager/DGM/GM/CXO before 15th January 2023.
