CEO's Circular No. 39/2022

CEO's Office, Sri Lanka Telecom PLC, Colombo 01.

30th April 2022

To: All SLT Staff,

DECLARING 2ND MAY 2022 AS A SPECIAL HOLIDAY FOR SLT STAFF

The Management has decided to declare 2nd May 2022 as a special holiday for SLT staff.

Further, 3rd May 2022 is also considered as a holiday for SLT employees as communicated via the CEO's Circular No: 41/2021 (Holidays for SLT Employees 2022) dated 13th September 2021.

However, the employees who have already been assigned for operational/essential duties on above days should report to work and the relevant payments due to them will be made accordingly.

Janaka R. Abeysinghe Chief Executive Officer
