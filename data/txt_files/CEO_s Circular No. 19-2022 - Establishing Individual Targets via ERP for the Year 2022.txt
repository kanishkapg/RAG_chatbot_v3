In the view of enhancing the performance evaluation management system, management has introduced certain improvements to the performance management system in 2021. The improvements will create a rewarding culture and encourage employees who are contributing positively to the growth of the organization. The objective of the revised system is to encourage the staff to drive the annual business targets and achieve objectives of the organization more effectively while delivering higher level of customer satisfaction.

The improved Performance Management System has Introduced KPI based rewarding scheme, peer evaluation mechanism, continuous follow up and feedback and Training & Development needs of the employee. Employee performance for the year has been reviewed against achievement of assigned KPI, which will be monitored carefully and reviewed throughout the year. The evaluation was conducted by a team of supervising officers and peers of the respective supervising officer in order to ensure a fair and transparent evaluation.

Please note that the effective period related to annual individual targets/KPIs for the year of 2022 is from 01st January 2022 to 31st December 2022.

All supervising officers are responsible on ensuring establishing individual KPIs for their subordinates in line with the company ABP. Therefore, all supervising officers are requested to ensure that the correct establishment of KPIs for their subordinates. Duly completed KPIs should be updated in the ERP system by relevant immediate supervisory officer and cascaded to subordinates.

Establishing Individual KPIs
Individual KPIs of all SLT staff should be set-up through ERP system (2022 SLT Performance Management Plan is published in the ERP system).

Nominating Supervisor's Peers
After completion of the KPI setting process, relevant supervising officer should nominate two peers for each employee for the purpose of year end performance appraisal. Relevant employee and the peers should be clearly communicated about the nomination and same should be updated in the ERP system as well.
Supervisor's peers are usually officers at the same level or rank work with the supervisor. Peers can be appointed within the group or outside the group. Accordingly peer may comprise of employees from same/different divisions/sections or teams that are working together as one unit for the completion of a project/target.

Guidelines
Performance management procedure document (which includes KPIs guidelines) and ERP user guide for KPI setting can be downloaded from the intranet. Please read complete performance management procedure document and ERP user guide prior to commencement of KPI setting process.

The following directions will guide you in establishing, achieving and monitoring targets & objectives for your staff:

- All Chief Officers are already established individual targets and objectives for their subordinates in line with Group/Divisional operational plan based on the company Annual Business Plan (ABP). Accordingly Group's ABP targets should be mapped into General Manager, Deputy General Manager and all staff reporting below them.
- Key Results Areas (KRAs)/Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) recommended by the Management/ABP for each group shall be taken into consideration when establishing individual KPIs.
- All supervising officers are responsible to establish KPIs to their subordinates. Supervising officers also should ensure update their subordinates' KPIs and assignment of supervisor peers in ERP system.
- CEO and Chief Officers will peer audit DGM and above officers to ensure cross functional alignment of ABP objectives with operational plans and staff responsibilities on KPIs.
- Targets/KPIs must be defined by considering the short term and long term goals of the group and they should be defined based on ABP 2022 targets and respective divisional & sectional operational plans should be linked to ABP 2022 targets.

- Individual targets/KPIs have to be SMART and linked to operational plan targets.
  - Specific (no ambiguity)
  - Measurable (in terms of time, number, volume or value)
  - Agreed (mutually agreed)
  - Realistic (can be particularly attainable, but only with an extra effort)
  - Time- bound (must have a time schedule)

- They should be defined as appropriate for each employee under the following key areas.
  - Customer
  - Financial
  - Internal Business Processes
  - Learning & Growth

- Achievement of individual KPIs should be monitored monthly by all reporting officers. Employees should discuss their KPI targets and progress with their reporting officers regularly (at least every month). These documents should be made available for management reference at any time.

- Reviewing Officers should monitor the progress of performance throughout the year. Progress and deviations (if any) should be monitored continuously and corrective measures should be taken with the help of respective reporting officers.

- Employees are not permitted to add, modify or omit targets/KPIs once they are approved by the management. Any changes should be done with instructions/guidance and proper approvals of the respective supervising officer.

- Closing date for completion of above process via ERP is on or before 15th April 2022.
